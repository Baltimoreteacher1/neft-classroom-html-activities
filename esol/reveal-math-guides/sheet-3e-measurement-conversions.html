📏 3E | Measurement Conversions Study Guide

Unit 3: Ratios & Proportions • Lesson 3.5 • Standard: 6.RP.3d

📘 Key Vocabulary

Convert — to change from one unit of measurement to another. (convertir)
Customary System — the measurement system used in the United States (inches, feet, pounds, gallons). (sistema inglés)
Metric System — the measurement system used in most of the world (meters, liters, grams). (sistema métrico)
Ratio Table — a table used to organize conversion factors and find equivalent measurements. (tabla de razones)
Unit — a standard amount used to measure (inch, foot, meter, cup, etc.). (unidad)

📋 Common Conversions Reference

Customary (U.S.) Length

From To Multiply by
1 foot (ft) 12 inches (in) × 12
1 yard (yd) 3 feet (ft) × 3
1 mile (mi) 5,280 feet (ft) × 5,280

Customary (U.S.) Capacity

From To Multiply by
1 cup (c) 8 fluid ounces (fl oz) × 8
1 pint (pt) 2 cups (c) × 2
1 quart (qt) 2 pints (pt) × 2
1 gallon (gal) 4 quarts (qt) × 4

Metric Length

From To Multiply by
1 kilometer (km) 1,000 meters (m) × 1,000
1 meter (m) 100 centimeters (cm) × 100
1 centimeter (cm) 10 millimeters (mm) × 10

Metric Mass

From To Multiply by
1 kilogram (kg) 1,000 grams (g) × 1,000
1 gram (g) 1,000 milligrams (mg) × 1,000

🔢 Step-by-Step — Using a Ratio Table to Convert

  1. 1 Write the conversion factor as a ratio (example: 1 foot = 12 inches).
  2. 2 Set up a ratio table with the known and unknown values.
  3. 3 Multiply or divide both parts by the same number to find the answer.
  4. 4 Label your answer with the correct unit!
Example: Convert 5 feet to inches.

Conversion: 1 foot = 12 inches

Feet 1 2 3 4 5
Inches 12 24 36 48 60

Or simply: 5 × 12 = 60 inches

Example: Convert 3,000 meters to kilometers.

Conversion: 1 km = 1,000 m → going from smaller to bigger, so divide.
3,000 ÷ 1,000 = 3 kilometers

📐 Formula Box

Bigger unit → Smaller unit = MULTIPLY by the conversion factor
Smaller unit → Bigger unit = DIVIDE by the conversion factor

💡 Memory Tip

Going to a smaller unit? The number gets BIGGER (more small pieces). Multiply.

Going to a bigger unit? The number gets SMALLER (fewer big pieces). Divide.

2 feet → ? inches (smaller unit) → 2 × 12 = 24 inches (bigger number)
36 inches → ? feet (bigger unit) → 36 ÷ 12 = 3 feet (smaller number)

⚠️ Watch Out!

  • Don't mix up multiply and divide! Remember: smaller unit = multiply, bigger unit = divide.
  • Metric is based on 10s (10, 100, 1000). Customary uses different numbers (12 inches/foot, 3 feet/yard, 4 quarts/gallon).
  • Always include the unit in your answer! "60" is incomplete. "60 inches" is correct.
  • Don't convert between systems unless given a conversion factor. You need to know the exchange (e.g., 1 inch = 2.54 cm) to go between customary and metric.

✏️ Practice Problems

Type just the number (no units needed).
