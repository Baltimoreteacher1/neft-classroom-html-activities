📈 Operations & Decimals

Divide fractions by multiplying by the reciprocal (Keep-Change-Flip). For decimals, align decimal places when adding or subtracting, and count spaces when multiplying.

Keeps-Change-Flip: Para dividir fracciones, multiplica por la recíproca.
a/b ÷ c/d = a/b × d/c

⚖️ Equations & Variables

Perform inverse operations on both sides to isolate the variable. Whatever operation you apply to one side, you must apply to the other to keep it balanced.

Operaciones Inversas: Despeja la variable aplicando la operación opuesta en ambos lados.
ax + b = c → ax = c - b

📊 Ratio & Coordinate Area

Determine coordinate distances by subtracting coordinates with matching axes, and build ratio tables to scale units evenly.

Escalar Razones: Multiplica o divide los términos por el mismo número para mantener proporciones.
A : B = (A × k) : (B × k)
🔄 Reciprocal
Recíproca: La fracción invertida.

The multiplicative inverse of a fraction (e.g. reciprocal of 2/3 is 3/2).

⚖️ Inverse Operation
Operación Inversa: La operación que deshace a otra.

Operations that undo each other (such as addition and subtraction, or multiplication and division).

🗺️ Coordinate Plane
Plano Cartesiano: Sistema de cuadrículas con ejes X e Y.

A 2D grid plane formed by horizontal X-axis and vertical Y-axis intersecting at origin (0,0).
