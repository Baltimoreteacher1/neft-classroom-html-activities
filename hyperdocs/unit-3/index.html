Ratio Rally: Rates, Unit Rates & Percent

Tune the rates to win. Learn, practice, and show what you know.

Learning Target

  • I can find the unit rate by dividing two quantities.
  • I can identify and create equivalent ratios.
  • I can calculate a percent of a number using multiplication.
Standard: CCSS 6.RP.A.2–3 Estimated time: 45–55 minutes Materials: This page, pencil or scratch paper, calculator (optional) Branding: Neft Teacher

3 · Explain Learn It

Ratio: compares two amounts. Example: 3 cups water to 1 cup juice = 3 : 1.

Rate: a ratio with different units. Example: 120 miles in 2 hours.

Unit rate: the rate for 1. Divide to get it.

Worked example — Unit rate

A car goes 120 miles in 2 hours.

120 ÷ 2 = 60. So the unit rate is 60 miles per hour.

Worked example — Percent

Find 25% of 80. Percent means "out of 100."

25% = 0.25, and 0.25 × 80 = 20.

WordEasy meaning
RateHow much of one thing for another (miles per hour)
Unit rateThe amount for just 1 (60 miles per 1 hour)
PercentA number out of 100 (50% = 50 out of 100)
Equivalent ratiosRatios that are equal (1:2 = 2:4)
