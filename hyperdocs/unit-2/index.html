Explain — Learn the Rule

Key idea: To divide by a fraction, flip the second fraction and multiply. The flipped fraction is called the reciprocal.

  • Dividing fractions — splitting a fraction into equal parts.
  • Reciprocal — flip a fraction. The reciprocal of 1/8 is 8/1 = 8.
  • Quotient — the answer to a division problem.
  • Keep–Flip–Multiply — Keep the first fraction. Flip the second. Multiply.

Worked example — the brownie question from Step 1:

3/4 ÷ 1/8

Keep: 3/4   Flip: 1/8 → 8/1   Multiply:

3/4 × 8/1 = 24/4 = 6

The baker can cut 6 servings. ✅
