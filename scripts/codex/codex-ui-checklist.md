# Student-Facing UI Checklist

- First screen makes the task obvious: title, directions, next action.
- Body text is at least 16px; directions and primary controls are larger.
- Contrast is strong in normal, hover, selected, disabled, and focus states.
- Buttons and links have clear labels; no icon-only controls without labels/tooltips.
- Keyboard navigation works and focus is visible.
- Layout works on Chromebook, tablet, and phone widths without clipping or horizontal scroll.
- Activity avoids clutter and keeps cognitive load low.
- Errors and recovery paths are clear: back, retry, edit, reset, print, or export.
- Save/export/print controls are real if shown.
- No placeholder text, fake data, hidden answer keys, or teacher-only content in student screens.
