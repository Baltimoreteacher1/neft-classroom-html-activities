# Education Content Checklist

- Content is grade-level appropriate and mathematically accurate.
- Directions are concise, chunked, and student-friendly.
- Key vocabulary is previewed or supported.
- WIDA/ESOL supports are present when helpful: visuals, word banks, sentence frames, examples.
- Spanish vocabulary/cognates are accurate and useful when included.
- TWR writing prompts include claim/evidence/reasoning or explain-your-thinking frames.
- Units, labels, examples, and answer choices are checked.
- Hints target misconceptions without giving away answers too early.
- Answer keys and teacher notes are separate from student-facing screens.
- Unsupported claims, confusing wording, and ambiguous questions are removed.
