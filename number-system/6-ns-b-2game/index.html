Submarine Division Command

Studio Edition v2 · 6.NS.B.2 · Multi-digit division using the standard algorithm

Mission Brief

Three underwater systems are failing. Steer your submarine into neutral sonar drones to select an answer. All drones look the same until you commit. Wrong collisions damage the hull and trigger smarter support: first a conceptual hint, then a procedural hint, then a worked-example rescue. Finish all zones to unlock the reactor core.

  • Keyboard: Arrow keys or WASD
  • Touch: on-screen movement pad
  • Correct answers build streak, XP, and zone progress
  • Feedback appears only after collision

Premium Systems

  • Mechanic-first gameplay loop
  • Neutral answer drone design
  • Animated ocean environment
  • Collision-based answer selection
  • Zone progression + reactor boss
  • Particles, camera shake, glow, and SFX
  • Support pipeline: concept → procedure → worked example

Mission Guide

Each division prompt spawns four neutral sonar drones in the water. Move into a drone to commit to that answer. Before collision, none of them reveal whether they are correct. On mistakes, the game keeps you on the same problem and adds support. After the third mistake, you complete the final step of a worked example before returning to the mission.

  • Zone 1: build strong quotient estimates with friendlier numbers
  • Zone 2: maintain place value and finish every step
  • Zone 3: handle larger dividends and zeros in the quotient
  • Boss: stabilize the reactor with one final high-value division repair

Worked Example Rescue

Complete the last step to recalibrate the system, then the mission continues.

Mission Complete

The submarine systems are stable. Division command restored.
