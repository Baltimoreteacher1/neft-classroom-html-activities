📚 Cartesian Guidelines

Review the absolute value and coordinate mapping rules to solve missions quickly.

🪞 The Reflection Rules - **Reflecting over the X-Axis**: The x-coordinate remains identical, but the y-coordinate sign flips to its opposite: $$(x, y) \xrightarrow{\text{Reflect } X} (x, -y)$$ - **Reflecting over the Y-Axis**: The y-coordinate remains identical, but the x-coordinate sign flips to its opposite: $$(x, y) \xrightarrow{\text{Reflect } Y} (-x, y)$$
📏 Coordinate Distances To calculate the distance between two coordinates with matching x or y coordinates: - **Different Quadrants**: Add the absolute values of the varying coordinates: $$d = |y_1| + |y_2| \quad \text{or} \quad |x_1| + |x_2|$$ - **Same Quadrants**: Subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger: $$d = |y_1| - |y_2| \quad \text{or} \quad |x_1| - |x_2|$$
