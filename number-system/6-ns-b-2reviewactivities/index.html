Worked Example — 432 ÷ 6
💡
The standard algorithm repeats four steps: Divide → Multiply → Subtract → Bring Down. Keep cycling until there are no digits left.
1
Divide
6 goes into 43 → 7 times
2
Multiply
7 × 6 = 42
3
Subtract
43 − 42 = 1
4
Bring Down
Bring 2 → 12
5
Divide Again
12 ÷ 6 = 2
✓ Quotient = 72
