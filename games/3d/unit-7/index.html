Submarine — Dive the Number Line

Your sub dives forward on its own. 0 is sea level. Hold Up / Down (or W / S or the d-pad) to set your depth on the number line. Work out the number the task asks for, then grab the GREEN token at that depth and dodge the red mines. Press Enter to hear your depth and its distance from 0.
