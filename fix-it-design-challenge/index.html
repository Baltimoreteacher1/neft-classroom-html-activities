2

The OSAMR framework

Use this to evaluate any AI scaffold. The goal is not the highest level — it is the right level for the learning target.

S

Substitution

Same task. AI replaces a tool.

A

Augmentation

Same task. AI adds useful support.

M

Modification

Task is redesigned, but the target stays intact.

R

Redefinition

AI enables a new learning move students could not easily do before.

O

Overreliance

AI does the thinking, answer, evidence, or product for students.

Model scenario · Grade 7 ELA

What went wrong?

Students read a challenging article and identify the author’s central claim with two pieces of evidence. The AI support made the article easier, but it also did the close-reading work.

Problem AI Input

“Summarize this article for 7th graders and tell students the author’s central claim and two pieces of evidence they should use in their response.”

Protect: Students must read the article, identify the claim, select evidence, and explain the connection.
