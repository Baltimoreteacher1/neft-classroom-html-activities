Chapter structure

  • Samuel escapes hanging and is taken to the orphanage.
  • Reverend Hunt teaches him about love, anger, and right choices.
  • Samuel’s fighting gets him chosen for the Virginia journey.
  • The chapter introduces the ships, Captain Smith, and the gentlemen.
  • Samuel ends still guarded, insisting he does not need a friend.
