Chapter Snapshot

A quick overview before students move into the scene-by-scene experience.

overview
SettingLondon, England, October 1606. Dark streets, pawnshop, docks, poorhouse memories, and London Bridge create a harsh atmosphere.
Main conflictSamuel steals back his mother’s locket, is pursued, and is eventually captured and threatened with hanging.
Key symbolThe silver locket represents Samuel’s mother, his grief, his memory, and his anger over what has been taken from him.
Chapter jobChapter 1 introduces Samuel’s hardship, explains his emotional motive, and ends with him in crisis.

Chapter structure

  • The prophecy opens with a historical warning.
  • Samuel breaks into the pawnshop and takes the locket.
  • The docks and poorhouse memories deepen character understanding.
  • London Bridge becomes temporary shelter.
  • Capture, threat, and blackout end the chapter in crisis.
