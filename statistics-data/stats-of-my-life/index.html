Visual Math Notes

What do these four important words mean?

Vocabulary Map

⚖️ Mean

The "Fair Share" average. Add all numbers, then divide by the count.

Promedio: Suma los números y divide por la cantidad.
🎯 Median

The middle number. Put numbers in order from least to greatest first!

Mediana: El número del medio en una lista ordenada.
🔄 Mode

The most common number. Look for the value that repeats most often.

Moda: El número que más se repite.
↔️ Range

The spread of data. Greatest value minus the smallest value.

Rango: El número mayor menos el número menor.

Quick Formulas

MEAN
Total Sum ÷ Count
MEDIAN
Order → Find Middle
MODE
Most Frequent
RANGE
Max - Min

Sentence Frames

Use these to talk about your data:

  • "The data shows ___ because ___."
    • "Los datos muestran ___ porque ___."
  • "My mean is higher than yours, which means ___."
    • "Mi promedio es más alto que el tuyo, lo que significa ___."
