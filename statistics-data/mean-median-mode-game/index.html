🕹 Player Guide

How to Win

Game Rules

Correct answer: earn points, attack the monster, and build a streak.
Wrong answer: lose a life unless your shield is active.
Streaks: 3 correct answers in a row unlock a super attack and bonus points.
Mini-games: clear them after rounds to earn extra power-ups.

Power-Ups

💡 Coach Hint: gives a strategy clue.
🙋 Ask Mr. Neft: shows a teacher-style strategy hint without giving away the answer.
🛡 Shield: blocks one monster hit.
📏 Sort Beam: orders the data chips to help with median and mode.
