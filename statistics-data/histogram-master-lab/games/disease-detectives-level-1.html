2 • Histogram Bootcamp (Optional)

Histogram or bar graph?

A histogram groups numbers into intervals. A bar graph compares categories.

Histogram: “How many countries are in each malaria-rate interval?” Example intervals: 0–49, 50–99, 100–149.

Bar graph: “How many students chose each disease topic?” Example categories: malaria, flu, measles.
