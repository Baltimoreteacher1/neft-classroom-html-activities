2 • Histogram Bootcamp (Optional)

Histogram or bar graph?

A histogram groups numbers into intervals. A bar graph compares categories.

Histogram: “How many players scored in each point interval?” Example intervals: 0–9, 10–19, 20–29.

Bar graph: “How many students picked each favorite game?” Example categories: Fortnite, Roblox, NBA 2K, Minecraft.

Sort:Click each card. Then click the correct box. Histograms use number ranges; bar graphs use categories.
