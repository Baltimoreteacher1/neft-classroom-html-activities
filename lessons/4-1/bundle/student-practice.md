## Words to Know
- **Ratio** _(es: Razón)_ — A comparison of two amounts. _(Una comparación de dos cantidades.)_
  - Example: 3 cups of flour to 2 cups of sugar = 3 : 2
- **Rate** _(es: Tasa)_ — A ratio that compares two different units. _(Una razón que compara dos unidades diferentes.)_
  - Example: 150 miles in 3 hours
- **Unit rate** _(es: Tasa unitaria)_ — A rate for exactly 1 of something. _(Una tasa para exactamente 1 de algo.)_
  - Example: 150 miles in 3 hours = 50 miles per hour
- **Per** _(es: Por)_ — The word that means 'for each one'. _(La palabra que significa 'por cada uno'.)_
  - Example: $0.50 per pencil

## Formulas
- unit rate = total amount ÷ number of units
- better buy = the lower price per unit

## Worked Examples
**Modeled Example 1.** 12 apples cost $6. Find the price per apple.

   1. A rate is $6 for 12 apples.
   2. Divide to get the price for 1 apple: 6 ÷ 12 = 0.5.
   3. So the unit rate is $0.50 per apple.

   **Answer:** $0.50 per apple

**Modeled Example 2.** A car travels 150 miles in 3 hours. Find the miles per hour.

   1. The rate is 150 miles in 3 hours.
   2. Divide to get miles for 1 hour: 150 ÷ 3 = 50.
   3. The unit rate is 50 miles per hour.

   **Answer:** 50 miles per hour
