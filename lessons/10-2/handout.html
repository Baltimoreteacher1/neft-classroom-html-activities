Volume of Rectangular Prisms

6.G.2 · Unit 10 · Lesson 2

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can find the volume of a rectangular prism, including ones with fractional edge lengths, using base area × height.

Key Idea / Idea clave

A fractional edge does not change the rule: still multiply length × width × height.

I Notice / Observo

  • What are the three measurements of the time capsule?
  • What does volume measure — length, area, or space inside?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • What if we made the capsule taller but narrower — would the volume change?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición
Volume
Volumen		How much space is inside a solid shape.
Cuánto espacio hay dentro de una figura sólida.
Rectangular prism
Prisma rectangular		A solid box shape with six flat rectangle sides.
Una figura sólida en forma de caja con seis lados rectangulares.
Cubic units
Unidades cúbicas		The units used to measure space inside, like cubic inches.
Las unidades para medir el espacio interior, como pulgadas cúbicas.
Dimensions
Dimensiones		How long, how wide, and how tall a shape is.
Qué tan largo, ancho y alto es una figura.
Net
Plantilla (desarrollo plano)		A flat shape that folds up into a solid.
Una figura plana que se dobla y forma un sólido.
Base area
Área de la base		The area of the bottom of a solid. Volume = base area × height.
El área del fondo de un sólido. Volumen = área de la base × altura.

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. A shipping container is 8 ft long, 4 ft wide, and 3 ft tall. It is half full of boxes.
  2. Find the missing dimension or volume for each container.
