Equivalent Ratios

6.RP.3 · Unit 3 · Lesson 4

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can find and check equivalent ratios using multiplication and division.

Key Idea / Idea clave

Multiply or divide both numbers by the same amount to make an equivalent ratio.

I Notice / Observo

  • What quantities are being compared in the recipe?
  • What stays the same when you multiply both ingredients by the same number?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • What if we needed to scale DOWN for just 4 people?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición
Ratio
Razón		A way to compare two amounts.
Una manera de comparar dos cantidades.
Equivalent ratios
Razones equivalentes		Two ratios that mean the same thing.
Dos razones que significan lo mismo.
Rate
Tasa		A ratio comparing two amounts with different units, like miles per hour.
Una razón que compara dos cantidades con unidades distintas, como millas por hora.
Proportion
Proporción		A math sentence saying two ratios are equal.
Una oración matemática que dice que dos razones son iguales.
Simplify
Simplificar		To make a ratio smaller while keeping the same comparison.
Hacer una razón más pequeña sin cambiar la comparación.

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. Sort these ratios — which are equivalent to 3:4 and which are not?
  2. Marcus earns $45 for every 3 hours of work. How much does he earn in 8 hours?
