Interpret Division of Fractions

6.NS.1 · Unit 2 · Lesson 1

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can use models to interpret what it means to divide by a fraction.

Key Idea / Idea clave

Dividing by a fraction smaller than 1 gives an answer BIGGER than what you started with, because many tiny pieces fit inside.

I Notice / Observo

  • What total length is being divided?
  • What is the size of each section?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • What does it mean to divide by a fraction?

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. What does 3 ÷ 1/4 mean?
    1. How many 1/4-size pieces fit into 3
    2. 3 groups of 1/4
    3. 3 minus 1/4
    4. 1/4 of 3
  2. Match each division expression to its quotient.
  3. Use the bar model to show how many 1/6-size pieces fit into 1 whole. Then determine how many fit into 3 wholes.
  4. Find the Interpretation Error
