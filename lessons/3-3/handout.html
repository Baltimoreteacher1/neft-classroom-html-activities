Graph Ratio Tables

6.RP.3a · Unit 3 · Lesson 3

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can graph the values from a ratio table as points on the coordinate plane.

Key Idea / Idea clave

The points from equal ratios form a straight line that passes through the origin (0, 0).

I Notice / Observo

  • What two quantities could we put on the x-axis and y-axis?
  • What pattern do you see in the ratio table values?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • What if the points did NOT form a straight line — what would that mean?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición
Coordinate plane
Plano cartesiano		A grid with a line going across and a line going up to plot points.
Una cuadrícula con una línea horizontal y una vertical para marcar puntos.
Ordered pair
Par ordenado		Two numbers (x, y) that tell where a point is on a grid.
Dos números (x, y) que dicen dónde está un punto en una cuadrícula.
Linear pattern
Patrón lineal		Points that make a straight line on a grid.
Puntos que forman una línea recta en una cuadrícula.
Proportional
Proporcional		Two amounts that grow together at the same rate.
Dos cantidades que crecen juntas al mismo ritmo.
Origin
Origen		The point (0, 0) where the two grid lines cross.
El punto (0, 0) donde se cruzan las dos líneas de la cuadrícula.

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. Chef Reyes uses 3 cups of flour for every 1 cup of sugar. Plot the ordered pairs from the ratio table: (1,3), (2,6), (3,9), (4,12).
  2. Points from a ratio table are plotted at (1, 4), (2, 8), and (3, 12). What is the ratio of y to x for each point?
