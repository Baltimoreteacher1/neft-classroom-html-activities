Vocabulary

Read each word, its meaning, and the example. These are the words you'll use in the lesson — learn them first, then practice.

💯

percent

Level 1 A number out of 100. The sign % means "out of 100."

Example: 50% means 50 out of 100.

🔘

decimal

Level 1 A number with a dot that shows parts smaller than one whole.

Example: 0.5 is a decimal.

🍕

fraction

Level 1 A number that shows part of a whole, with a top and bottom number.

Example: 1/2 is a fraction.

🟰

equivalent

Level 1 Having the same value, even if it looks different.

Example: 1/2, 0.5, and 50% are equivalent.

Learn It

Three ways, one amount

Percent means "out of 100." So a percent is just a fraction with 100 on the bottom, which is also an easy decimal.

50% = 50/100 = 0.5 = 1/2 — all the same half.
25% = 25/100 = 0.25 = 1/4. To go from percent to decimal, slide the dot 2 places: 25% → 0.25.

Hint

Hint

25/100 simplifies to 1/4.

Level 2 Stretch your conversions.

A. Write 1/4 as a percent.

Hint

1/4 = 25/100 = 25%.

Hint

0.75 = 75/100 = 75% = 3/4.
