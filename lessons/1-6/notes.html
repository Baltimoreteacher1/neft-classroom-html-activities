Key Vocabulary Level 1 support

Picture first, then the word, then a plain-language meaning. Say each word out loud.

4 × 7 = 28, so the product is 28

Product

The answer to a multiplication problem

In 3.25, the decimal point separates 3 (ones) from 25 (tenths and hundredths)

Decimal point

The dot that separates the whole number part from the fractional part

3.7 × 4.2: round to 4 × 4 = 16, so the exact answer should be near 16

Estimate

An approximate answer found by rounding before computing

In 0.36: the 3 is in the tenths place (0.3) and the 6 is in the hundredths place (0.06)

Place value

The value of a digit based on its position in a number

3.4 (1 place) × 2.6 (1 place) = 8.84 (2 places total)

Decimal places

The number of digits to the right of the decimal point; used to place the decimal in a product

Key Ideas & Notes

  • The station needs 4.5 meters of heat shielding that costs $12.60 per meter.
  • Commander Diaz needs to calculate the total cost before submitting the supply order.
  • What is the total cost of the shielding?
  • Estimate each product by rounding, then sort: which products are GREATER than 20 and which are LESS than 20?

Think About It

  • What two quantities are being multiplied?
  • How many total decimal places do the two factors have?
  • How can estimating help you check if your answer is reasonable?

Example 1

What is 3.4 × 2.6?

Solution: 34 × 26 = 884. There are 2 total decimal places (1 + 1), so place the decimal to get 8.84.

Answer: A. 8.84

Example 2

Answer: A. 1.05

Example 3

Solution: 53 × 4 = 212. There is 1 total decimal place, so place the decimal to get 21.2.

Answer: A. 21.2
