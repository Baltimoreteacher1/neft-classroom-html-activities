Fraction Division Problem Solving

6.NS.1 · Unit 2 · Lesson 5

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can solve real-world problems by writing and solving fraction division equations.

Key Idea / Idea clave

Total goes first (dividend), group size goes second (divisor): total ÷ group size. Then check your answer by multiplying it back.

I Notice / Observo

  • What is the total amount of fingerprint dust?
  • How much does each test use?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • How do you decide which number is the dividend and which is the divisor?

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. A ribbon is 4/5 of a yard long. Each bow uses 1/10 of a yard. Which equation finds how many bows can be made?
    1. 4/5 ÷ 1/10
    2. 1/10 ÷ 4/5
    3. 4/5 × 1/10
    4. 4/5 + 1/10
  2. Solve each word problem by writing and solving an equation.
  3. Sort: is the equation and solution CORRECT, or is there an error?
  4. Find the Problem-Solving Error
