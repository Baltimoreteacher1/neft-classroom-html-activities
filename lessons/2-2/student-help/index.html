I can…

I can divide a whole number by a fraction by multiplying by the reciprocal.

Key words

  • Whole Number — A counting number with no fraction or decimal, like 0, 1, 2, 3.
  • Fraction — A number that shows part of a whole, like 3/4.
  • Reciprocal — A fraction turned upside down.
  • Keep, Change, Flip — A way to divide fractions: keep the first, change ÷ to ×, flip the second.
  • Quotient — The answer when you divide.

Steps

  1. Agent Park has 4 pounds of powder and each bag holds 1/2 pound. I want 4 ÷ 1/2.
  2. Keep the 4. Change ÷ to ×. Flip 1/2 to make 2/1, which is just 2.
  3. Now I multiply: 4 × 2 = 8.
  4. So 4 ÷ 1/2 = 8. He can fill 8 bags. That makes sense: each pound fills 2 bags, and 4 pounds × 2 = 8.
