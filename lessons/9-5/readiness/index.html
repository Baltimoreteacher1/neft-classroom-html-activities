Learn It

x is across, y is up or down

Every point is an ordered pair (x, y). The x is across: right or left. The y is vertical: up or down. Left and right are opposites, and up and down are opposites — that is the only new idea for all four quadrants.

(2, 5): 2 right, 5 up.
Opposites: if right is the positive direction, then left is the opposite. If up is positive, down is the opposite.

Level 1 Read ordered pairs.

A. A point is 5 right and 1 up. What is its ordered pair?

Hint

x first (5), then y (1).

B. What is the opposite of up?

Hint

Level 2 Stretch into opposite directions.

A. Point P is 4 right of 0. Its mirror is the same distance the opposite way. How far left is the mirror?

Hint

Same distance, opposite direction: 4 left.

B. To plot (6, 4), how far do you move up (y)?

Hint

y is the second number: 4.
