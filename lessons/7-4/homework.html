Less than

💡 Example: 2 < 7 — two is smaller than seven

At least / At most

Al menos / A lo más

💡 Example: 'At least 10 players' → p ≥ 10 (could be 10, 11, 12, ...); 'At most 5 tries' → t ≤ 5 (could be 5, 4, 3, ...)
Tap to show definition ➔

'At least' means ≥. 'At most' means ≤.

'Al menos' significa ≥. 'Como máximo' significa ≤.

📌 'At least 10 players' → p ≥ 10 (could be 10, 11, 12, ...); 'At most 5 tries' → t ≤ 5 (could be 5, 4, 3, ...)

Variable

Variable

A letter that stands for an unknown number.

Una letra que representa un número desconocido.

📌 In x > 5, the letter x stands for any number greater than 5.

No more than

No más de

A phrase meaning less than or equal to (≤).

Problem 10
ERROR ANALYSIS

⚠️ Level 2 Extension — Catch the Symbol Mix-Up

Review the steps below. Identify which step contains the error, and explain why.

👪 Family tip: Read each step aloud and ask: does this step follow the math rules we learned?

Step 1 Clue: The suspect is at least 12 years on the force, where y is the years.
Step 2 Find the key phrase: 'At least 12' means 12 is allowed and anything larger.
Step 3 Write the inequality: y > 12
Correct Work Reference: 'At least 12' includes 12 itself, so the symbol must be ≥, not >. Using > would leave out exactly 12 years. The correct inequality is y ≥ 12.
