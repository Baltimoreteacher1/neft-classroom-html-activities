Write Inequalities

6.EE.8 · Unit 7 · Lesson 4

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can write inequalities to represent real-world situations.

Key Idea / Idea clave

'At least' means ≥ and 'at most' means ≤; 'more than' means > and 'fewer/less than' means <.

I Notice / Observo

  • What does 'at least 18' mean about the suspect's age?
  • Could the suspect be exactly 18?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • What if the witness said the suspect was less than 30 years old?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición

Greater than
Mayor que		The > sign, showing one number is bigger.
El signo >, que muestra que un número es mayor.
Less than

At least / At most

'Al menos' significa ≥. 'Como máximo' significa ≤.
Variable

Una letra que representa un número desconocido.
No more than
No más de		A phrase meaning less than or equal to (≤).
Una frase que significa menor o igual que (≤).

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. Match each situation to its inequality
  2. Identify the key phrase, symbol, and write the inequality for each situation.
