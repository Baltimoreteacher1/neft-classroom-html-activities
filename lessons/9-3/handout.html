Compare and Order Integers

6.NS.7 · Unit 9 · Lesson 3

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can compare and order integers using a number line.

Key Idea / Idea clave

The number farther to the left is always less; the number farther to the right is always greater.

I Notice / Observo

  • Which treasure is closest to the surface?
  • Is -7 greater than or less than -2?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • Why does a bigger negative number mean a deeper location?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición
Compare
Comparar		To see if a number is bigger, smaller, or equal to another.
Ver si un número es mayor, menor o igual que otro.
Order
Ordenar		To put numbers in order, smallest to biggest or biggest to smallest.
Poner números en orden, de menor a mayor o de mayor a menor.
Greater than
Mayor que		Farther right on a number line. The > sign.
Más a la derecha en la recta numérica. El signo >.
Less than
Menor que		Farther left on a number line. The < sign.
Más a la izquierda en la recta numérica. El signo <.
Negative integer
Entero negativo		A whole number smaller than zero, like -5.
Un número entero menor que cero, como -5.
Integer
Número entero		Whole numbers and their opposites, like -2, -1, 0, 1, 2.
Números enteros y sus opuestos, como -2, -1, 0, 1, 2.

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. Plot these integers and write them in order from greatest to least: -8, 4, -3, 1, -1, 6
  2. Compare each pair of integers using < , > , or =.
