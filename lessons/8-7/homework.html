🔑 Key Vocabulary

Tap a card to flip between the math term and kid-friendly definition. Pictures help families talk about the idea together.

Symmetric

Simétrico

💡 Example: Heights: 60, 62, 64, 66, 68, 66, 64, 62, 60 — rises and falls evenly like a hill
Tap to show definition ➔

Data that looks about the same on the left and right.

Datos que se ven casi iguales a la izquierda y a la derecha.

📌 Heights: 60, 62, 64, 66, 68, 66, 64, 62, 60 — rises and falls evenly like a hill

Skewed

Sesgado

💡 Example: Skewed right: most data low (1,2,2,3,3,3,15) — tail stretches toward the high value
Tap to show definition ➔

Data bunched on one side with a tail on the other.

Datos amontonados de un lado con una cola del otro.

📌 Skewed right: most data low (1,2,2,3,3,3,15) — tail stretches toward the high value

Cluster

Agrupamiento

💡 Example: Scores 18, 19, 20, 21, 22 form a cluster around 20 — they are tightly grouped
Tap to show definition ➔

A group of numbers that are close together.

Un grupo de números que están cerca unos de otros.

📌 Scores 18, 19, 20, 21, 22 form a cluster around 20 — they are tightly grouped

Gap

Hueco (espacio)

💡 Example: Data: 5, 6, 7, 8, ___, ___, ___, 20 — the empty space from 9 to 19 is a gap
Tap to show definition ➔

A big empty space where there is no data.

Un espacio grande y vacío donde no hay datos.

📌 Data: 5, 6, 7, 8, ___, ___, ___, 20 — the empty space from 9 to 19 is a gap

Data distribution

Distribución de datos

💡 Example: Looking at a dot plot or histogram, you can see if data is symmetric, skewed, clustered, or has gaps
Tap to show definition ➔

How the data looks: where it sits and how spread out it is.

Cómo se ven los datos: dónde están y qué tan separados están.

📌 Looking at a dot plot or histogram, you can see if data is symmetric, skewed, clustered, or has gaps

Variability

Variabilidad

💡 Example: Symmetric data with low variability: tightly packed around the center. Skewed data: spread unevenly
Tap to show definition ➔

How spread out the numbers are.

Qué tan separados están los números.

📌 Symmetric data with low variability: tightly packed around the center. Skewed data: spread unevenly
