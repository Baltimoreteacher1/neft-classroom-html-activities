Concept Introduction

💡 What shape is the data?

The shape of a data distribution describes how the values are spread out. Data can be symmetric (the same on both sides), skewed (bunched on one side with a long tail on the other), or have clusters and gaps.

Key Idea:

Symmetric data mirrors around the center; skewed data has a long tail, and the skew is named for the direction the tail points.

SYMMETRIC

Simétrico

Click to flip ➔

Data that looks about the same on the left and right.

Datos que se ven casi iguales a la izquierda y a la derecha.

Ex: Heights: 60, 62, 64, 66, 68, 66, 64, 62, 60 — rises and falls evenly like a hill
📝 A distribution where both sides look like mirror images is ___.

SKEWED

Sesgado

Click to flip ➔

Data bunched on one side with a tail on the other.

Datos amontonados de un lado con una cola del otro.

Ex: Skewed right: most data low (1,2,2,3,3,3,15) — tail stretches toward the high value
📝 A distribution with a tail stretching out to one side is ___.

CLUSTER

Agrupamiento

Click to flip ➔

A group of numbers that are close together.

Un grupo de números que están cerca unos de otros.

Ex: Scores 18, 19, 20, 21, 22 form a cluster around 20 — they are tightly grouped
📝 A group of data values bunched close together is a ___.

GAP

Hueco (espacio)

Click to flip ➔

A big empty space where there is no data.

Un espacio grande y vacío donde no hay datos.

Ex: Data: 5, 6, 7, 8, ___, ___, ___, 20 — the empty space from 9 to 19 is a gap
📝 An interval where there are no data values is a ___.

⚠️ ERROR ANALYSIS

Find the Shape Error

Analyze the worked steps on the clipboard. Hover over a step button below to highlight its line, select the incorrect step, and justify your fix.

Data: Sprint times (seconds): 10.2, 10.5, 10.8, 11.0, 11.1, 11.2, 11.3, 11.5, 14.8
Histogram: 10.0–10.9: 3, 11.0–11.9: 5, 12.0–12.9: 0, 13.0–13.9: 0, 14.0–14.9: 1
Shape: Skewed left because the tall bar is on the right side
Best measure: Use the mean because skewed data uses the mean
Identify Error Step:

🛠️ Fix & Justify

Correct Work: This is skewed RIGHT, not left. The cluster of data is on the left (10–11 seconds) and the tail with the extreme value (14.8) stretches to the right. Also, for skewed data the MEDIAN is the better measure, not the mean, because the mean is pulled toward the tail.
