Divide Fractions

6.NS.1 · Unit 2 · Lesson 3

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can divide a fraction by a fraction by multiplying by the reciprocal and simplifying.

Key Idea / Idea clave

To divide fractions, multiply the first fraction by the reciprocal of the second, then simplify the answer.

I Notice / Observo

  • What is the total weight being divided?
  • What is the size of each portion?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • How is dividing fractions different from multiplying them?
