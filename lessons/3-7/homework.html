Problem 10
ERROR ANALYSIS

⚠️ Level 2 Extension — Catch the Recipe Mistake

Review the steps below. Identify which step contains the error, and explain why.

👪 Family tip: Read each step aloud and ask: does this step follow the math rules we learned?

Step 1 Read the problem: 9 cups of batter fill 12 muffin cups. How many cups of batter are needed for 16 muffin cups?
Step 2 Set up the unit rate: 12 muffins ÷ 9 cups = about 1.33 muffins per cup
Step 3 Multiply for 16 muffins: 1.33 × 16 = about 21.3 cups
Step 4 Answer: About 21.3 cups of batter are needed
Correct Work Reference: The unit rate was set up in the wrong order. Divide cups by muffins: 9 cups ÷ 12 muffins = 0.75 cup per muffin. Then 0.75 × 16 = 12 cups of batter.
