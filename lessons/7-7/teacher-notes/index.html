Warm-up

Clue: 'The lookout earned less than $50.' Equation or inequality?

Teaching notes

Key idea: 'Equals' or 'exactly' points to an equation (=); 'at least, at most, more than, fewer than' points to an inequality.

I do:

  1. Clue: 'A total divided equally among 4 people gave each $85.'
  2. This has an exact total, so I use an equation. Let t be the total.
  3. Divided among 4 to get 85 means t ÷ 4 = 85, so t = 85 × 4 = 340.
  4. Check reasonableness: $340 split 4 ways is $85 each. That makes sense!

You do: In the table, decide equation or inequality, write the model, solve, and check it is reasonable. Then work through the practice problems on your own.

Common misconception

A common mistake in Equations and Inequalities Problem Solving is skipping the key idea: "'Equals' or 'exactly' points to an equation (=); 'at least, at most, more than, fewer than' points to an inequality." — always check your work against this rule before you submit.

ESOL / SPED supports

  • Pre-teach: Model, Equation, Inequality, Reasonableness, Variable, Constraint (Spanish + visuals are in the lesson vocabulary).
  • Offer the sentence frames: “I know ___ because ___.” “First, I ___. Then, I ___.” “The answer is ___, so ___.”
  • Chunk the I-do steps; let students restate each step to a partner before moving on.
  • Provide the worked example as a reference while they practice.

Exit ticket

A box of donuts has some donuts. After giving away 7, there are fewer than 5 left. Which inequality represents the starting number of donuts d?

  • d − 7 < 5 ✓ correct
  • d + 7 < 5
  • d − 7 > 5
  • d − 7 = 5

Starting with d donuts and giving away 7 leaves fewer than 5: d − 7 < 5. Solve: d < 12. ✓

Reteach

Re-run the I-do example with smaller numbers and have students narrate each step using the key idea.

Extension

A detective has a $500 budget for equipment. She has already spent $320. The inequality 320 + r ≤ 500 represents the remaining amount r she can spend. She also needs exactly 4 flashlights at $15 each, represented by the equation 4f = 60.
