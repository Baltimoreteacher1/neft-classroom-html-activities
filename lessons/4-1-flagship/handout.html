Rates and Unit Rates

6.RP.2 · Unit 4 · Lesson 1

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can find a unit rate to compare prices and decide the better buy.

Key Idea / Idea clave

To find a unit rate, divide the total cost by the number of items. The lower cost per item is the better buy.

I Notice / Observo

  • What two quantities are being compared at each booth?
  • Which booth seems like a better deal at first glance?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • How can you compare prices when the number of games is different?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición
Rate
Tasa		A ratio comparing two amounts with different units, like miles per hour.
Una razón que compara dos cantidades con unidades distintas, como millas por hora.
Unit Rate
Tasa unitaria		A rate for just 1 of something, like cost for 1 item.
Una tasa para solo 1 de algo, como el precio de 1 artículo.
Per
Por		For each one. Example: 5 dollars per book.
Por cada uno. Ejemplo: 5 dólares por libro.
Ratio
Razón		A way to compare two amounts.
Una manera de comparar dos cantidades.
Better Buy
Mejor compra		The choice that costs less for each item.
La opción que cuesta menos por cada artículo.
Divide
Dividir		Splitting a total into equal groups.
Repartir un total en grupos iguales.
