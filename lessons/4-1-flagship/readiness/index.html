Level 1 A way to compare two amounts that have different units, like miles and hours.

Learn It

What is a unit rate?

The word "per" means for each one. A unit rate is the amount for exactly 1. To find it, divide by how many groups there are.

$6 for 3 pens → 6 ÷ 3 = $2 per pen (the cost for ONE pen).
40 miles in 2 hours → 40 ÷ 2 = 20 miles per hour (distance in ONE hour).
