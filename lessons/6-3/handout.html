Write Algebraic Expressions

6.EE.2a · Unit 6 · Lesson 3

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can write algebraic expressions from words and real-world situations.

Key Idea / Idea clave

Words like "each" or "per" mean multiply, and "plus" or "more than" mean add.

I Notice / Observo

  • What value changes depending on how many people come?
  • What stays the same no matter the number of tickets?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • What if there were two different ticket prices — regular and VIP?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición
Variable
Variable		A letter that stands for a number that is unknown or can change.
Una letra que representa un número desconocido o que puede cambiar.
Algebraic Expression
Expresión algebraica		A math phrase that has at least one letter.
Una frase matemática que tiene al menos una letra.
Coefficient
Coeficiente		The number in front of a letter, like the 3 in 3x.
El número frente a una letra, como el 3 en 3x.
Constant
Constante		A number on its own that does not change.
Un número solo que no cambia.
Like terms
Términos semejantes		Terms with the same letter, like 2x and 5x.
Términos con la misma letra, como 2x y 5x.

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. Match each word phrase to its correct algebraic expression.
  2. Match each word phrase to its algebraic expression.
