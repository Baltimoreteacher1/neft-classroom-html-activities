Find the Percent of a Number

6.RP.3c · Unit 4 · Lesson 4

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can find the percent of a number using an equation or a model.

Key Idea / Idea clave

To find the part, change the percent to a decimal, then multiply: part = percent × base.

I Notice / Observo

  • What information do you need to find the discount amount?
  • How is finding 25% of 60 different from finding 40% of 80?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • Is there more than one way to find a percent of a number?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición
Percent
Porcentaje		A way to compare a number to 100, shown with the % sign.
Una manera de comparar un número con 100, con el signo %.
Base
Base (el todo)		The whole amount you take a percent of.
La cantidad total de la que sacas un porcentaje.
Part
Parte		The piece you get when you take a percent of a number.
La parte que obtienes al sacar un porcentaje de un número.
Equation
Ecuación		A math sentence with an equal sign showing both sides are the same.
Una oración matemática con un signo igual que muestra que ambos lados son iguales.
Discount
Descuento		Money taken off the first price to make it cheaper.
Dinero que se quita del precio original para que sea más barato.

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. Complete each row to find the percent of a number.
  2. An arcade prize costs 240 tickets. If you have a coupon for 15% off, how many tickets do you save?
