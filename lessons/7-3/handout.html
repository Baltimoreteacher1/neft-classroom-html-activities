Solve Multiplication and Division Equations

6.EE.7 · Unit 7 · Lesson 3

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can solve one-step multiplication and division equations using inverse operations.

Key Idea / Idea clave

Divide to undo multiplying, and multiply to undo dividing — always on both sides.

I Notice / Observo

  • What does the 3 represent in the equation?
  • What does x represent?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • What if there were 5 boxes instead of 3?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición
Inverse operation
Operación inversa		Two math actions that undo each other, like × and ÷.
Dos operaciones que se deshacen entre sí, como × y ÷.
Multiply
Multiplicar		To add equal groups again and again.
Sumar grupos iguales una y otra vez.
Divide
Dividir		To split into equal groups.
Repartir en grupos iguales.
Isolate
Despejar		To get the letter by itself on one side.
Dejar la letra sola en un lado.
Coefficient
Coeficiente		The number multiplied by a variable.
El número que multiplica a una variable.
Solution
Solución		The value of the variable that makes the equation true.
El valor de la variable que hace verdadera la ecuación.

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. Solve each equation, show your work, and check.
  2. A detective splits 54 clues equally among 9 case files. The equation 9c = 54 represents this. What is c?
