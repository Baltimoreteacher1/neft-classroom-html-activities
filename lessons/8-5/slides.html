Concept Launch

💡 What is a box plot?

👩‍🏫 Say: This is the big idea for today. Students should be able to repeat it by the end.

A box plot is a simple picture that shows how a set of data is spread out, using just five key numbers: the lowest value, the first quartile (Q1), the median (middle), the third quartile (Q3), and the highest value.

Key Idea:

The box always holds the middle 50% of the data, and the line inside the box is the median.

🎯 I can make and read a box plot to summarize a data set.
🏀 SPORTS ANALYTICS

Watch me build one

👩‍🏫 Say: Reveal one step at a time. Pause after each step for students to process.
⏱️ Time: 3–4 min
My data, in order: 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14, 15, 18, 22, 24, 28. There are 11 numbers.
The middle number is the 6th one, 14, so the median = 14.
The lower half is 4, 6, 8, 10, 12. Its middle is 8, so Q1 = 8.
The upper half is 15, 18, 22, 24, 28. Its middle is 22, so Q3 = 22.
The lowest is 4 and the highest is 28. I draw a box from 8 to 22, a line at 14, and whiskers out to 4 and 28.

Visual Vocabulary

Box Plot

Diagrama de caja

A graph that shows how data is spread using five key numbers.

Una gráfica que muestra cómo se reparten los datos con cinco números clave.

Example: A box from Q1 to Q3 with a line at the median, and whiskers from min to max
Use in context: A graph that shows data with a box and whiskers based on the five-number summary is a ___.
👩‍🏫 Say: Say the term, definition, and example. Students repeat.
🎯 I can make and read a box plot to summarize a data set.
🏀 SPORTS ANALYTICS

Visual Vocabulary

Median

Mediana

The middle number that splits the data in half.

El número del medio que divide los datos en dos mitades.

Example: Data: 10, 15, 20, 25, 30 → median is 20 (the 3rd value)
Use in context: The middle value of the data, shown as a line inside the box, is the ___.
👩‍🏫 Say: Say the term, definition, and example. Students repeat.
🎯 I can make and read a box plot to summarize a data set.
🏀 SPORTS ANALYTICS

Visual Vocabulary

Quartile

Cuartil

Numbers that split the data into four equal parts.

Números que dividen los datos en cuatro partes iguales.

Example: Data: 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12, 14 → Q1 = 4 (median of lower half), Q3 = 12 (median of upper half)
Use in context: A value that divides the data into four equal parts is a ___.
👩‍🏫 Say: Say the term, definition, and example. Students repeat.
🎯 I can make and read a box plot to summarize a data set.
🏀 SPORTS ANALYTICS

Visual Vocabulary

Interquartile Range

Rango intercuartílico

The spread of the middle half of the data (Q3 − Q1).

La extensión de la mitad central de los datos (Q3 − Q1).

Example: If Q1 = 12 and Q3 = 20, then IQR = 20 − 12 = 8 — the middle 50% spans 8 units
Use in context: The distance between the first and third quartiles, showing the middle 50% of data, is the ___.
👩‍🏫 Say: Say the term, definition, and example. Students repeat.
🎯 I can make and read a box plot to summarize a data set.

🎯 Listen For · Common Errors

• Students name the five-number summary (min, Q1, median, Q3, max) and find the median (14) for the 11 ordered scores.

• A strong answer explains the box covers the middle 50% (Q1 to Q3), its length is the IQR, and a longer box means greater spread.
