Solve and Graph Inequalities

6.EE.5 · Unit 7 · Lesson 6

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can solve an inequality and graph its solution set on a number line.

Key Idea / Idea clave

Solve with inverse operations, then graph the solution set: open circle for < or >, closed for ≤ or ≥.

I Notice / Observo

  • What is the unknown in this situation?
  • What operation do you need to undo?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • What if the total was at most $53 instead of more than $53?

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. Solve: x + 6 > 14
    1. x > 8
    2. x > 20
    3. x < 8
    4. x = 8
  2. Solve each inequality, describe the inverse operation, and describe the graph.
  3. Is x = 5 a solution to x + 4 > 12?
    1. No, because 5 + 4 = 9, and 9 is not greater than 12
    2. Yes, because 5 + 4 = 9
    3. Yes, because 5 > 4
    4. No, because 5 < 12
  4. Find the Solving Error
