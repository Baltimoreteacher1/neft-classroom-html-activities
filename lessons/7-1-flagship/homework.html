Write Equations

Target I can write an equation to represent a real-world situation.
Discuss I can explain my equation using the words equation, variable, equal sign, and expression.

🔑 Key Vocabulary

Equation

Ecuación

💡 Example: x + 5 = 12 means 'some number plus 5 equals 12'
Tap to show definition ➔

A math sentence with an equal sign showing both sides are the same.

Una oración matemática con un signo igual que muestra que ambos lados son iguales.

Variable

Variable

💡 Example: In n + 3 = 10, the letter n stands for the unknown number (n = 7)
Tap to show definition ➔

A letter that stands for an unknown number.

Una letra que representa un número desconocido.

Equal sign

Signo igual

💡 Example: 7 + 3 = 10 — both sides equal 10
Tap to show definition ➔

The = sign, showing both sides are the same.

El signo =, que muestra que ambos lados son iguales.

Expression

Expresión

💡 Example: 2x + 5 is an expression; it becomes an equation when you write 2x + 5 = 15
Tap to show definition ➔

A math phrase with numbers and letters, but no equal sign.

Una frase matemática con números y letras, pero sin signo igual.

Problem 5
ERROR ANALYSIS

⚠️ Find the Equation Error

Review the steps below. Identify which step contains the error, and explain why.

Step 1 Situation: Twice a number is 16
Step 2 Identify operation: Twice means addition
Step 3 Write equation: n + 2 = 16
Correct Work Reference: 'Twice' means multiply by 2, not add 2. The correct equation is 2n = 16.
