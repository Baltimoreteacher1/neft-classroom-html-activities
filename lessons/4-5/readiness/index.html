Learn It

Percents in word problems

Find the percent first, then decide whether to add it on (a tip) or subtract it off (a discount).

10% tip on $30: 10% of 30 = 3, then add → $33 total.
25% off $40: 25% of 40 = 10, then subtract → $30 sale price.

Practice · Your Level

Try It

Your quick check picks one for you, but you can switch any time:

Level 0 One small step at a time.

A. 10% of 20 = ___ (20 ÷ 10).

Hint

20 ÷ 10 = 2.

B. Now add it on: 20 + 2 = ___?

Hint

20 + 2 = 22.

C. Take it off instead: 20 − 2 = ___?

Hint

20 − 2 = 18.

Level 1 Find the percent, then add or subtract.

A. 50% of 60 = ___?

Hint

Half of 60 is 30.

B. A $40 shirt, take 25% off (25% of 40 = 10). Sale price = 40 − 10 = ___?

Hint

40 − 10 = 30.

C. A $50 meal, add a 10% tip (10% of 50 = 5). Total = 50 + 5 = ___?

Hint

50 + 5 = 55.

Level 2 Full mini word problems.

A. A $80 bike is 25% off. How many dollars do you SAVE? (25% of 80)

Hint

80 ÷ 4 = 20 saved.

B. 10% tip on a $90 bill, then the TOTAL = 90 + (10% of 90) = ___?

Hint

10% of 90 = 9, so 90 + 9 = 99.
