Powers and Exponents

Target I can write and evaluate numbers in exponent form using a base and a power.
Discuss I can explain my work using the words exponent, base, power, and evaluate.

🔑 Key Vocabulary

Exponent

Exponente

💡 Example: In 2³, the small 3 means multiply 2 by itself 3 times: 2 × 2 × 2 = 8
Tap to show definition ➔

A small number that tells how many times to multiply the number by itself.

Un número pequeño que dice cuántas veces multiplicar el número por sí mismo.

Base

Base

💡 Example: In 5², the base is 5 — it is the number being multiplied: 5 × 5 = 25
Tap to show definition ➔

The number that gets multiplied by itself.

El número que se multiplica por sí mismo.

Power

Potencia

💡 Example: 10³ = 10 × 10 × 10 = 1,000 — read as '10 to the third power' or '10 cubed'
Tap to show definition ➔

A number written with a base and an exponent, like 2³.

Un número escrito con una base y un exponente, como 2³.

Evaluate

Evaluar

💡 Example: Evaluate 3⁴: write 3 × 3 × 3 × 3, then multiply step by step: 9 × 9 = 81
Tap to show definition ➔

To find the value of an expression.

Encontrar el valor de una expresión.

Coefficient

Coeficiente

💡 Example: In 3x, the 3 is the coefficient — if x = 4, then 3x = 3 × 4 = 12
Tap to show definition ➔

The number in front of a letter, like the 3 in 3x.

El número frente a una letra, como el 3 en 3x.

Like terms

Términos semejantes

💡 Example: 4x and 2x are like terms (both have x); 4x and 4x² are NOT like terms (different powers)
Tap to show definition ➔

Terms with the same letter, like 2x and 5x.

Términos con la misma letra, como 2x y 5x.
