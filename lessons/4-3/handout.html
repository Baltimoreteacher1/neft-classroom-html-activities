Percents Greater Than 100% and Less Than 1%

6.RP.3c · Unit 4 · Lesson 3

Name   Period

Today's Goal / Meta de hoy

I can write and interpret percents greater than 100% and less than 1%.

Key Idea / Idea clave

To change any percent to a decimal, divide by 100 (move the decimal point two places to the left).

I Notice / Observo

  • What does it mean when a score is shown as 150% of the target?
  • Can a percent be less than 1%? What would that look like?

I Wonder / Me pregunto

  • How do you write a percent greater than 100% as a decimal or fraction?

Vocabulary / Vocabulario

Term / TérminoDefinition / Definición
Percent
Porcentaje		A way to compare a number to 100, shown with the % sign.
Una manera de comparar un número con 100, con el signo %.
Greater than 100%
Mayor que 100%		A percent bigger than 100%, more than the whole thing.
Un porcentaje mayor que 100%, más que todo el entero.
Less than 1%
Menor que 1%		A percent smaller than 1%, a very tiny part.
Un porcentaje menor que 1%, una parte muy pequeña.
Equivalent
Equivalente		Having the same value, just written a different way.
Tener el mismo valor, escrito de otra forma.
Mixed number
Número mixto		A whole number plus a fraction, like 1 1/2.
Un número entero más una fracción, como 1 1/2.

Practice Preview / Vista previa de práctica

  1. Which of the following is greater than 100%?
    1. 0.75
    2. 3/4
    3. 1.25
    4. 99/100
  2. Match each value to its equivalent percent.
  3. A player scored 0.4% of the bonus points. Which decimal represents this percent?
    1. 0.4
    2. 0.04
    3. 0.004
    4. 4.0
  4. Find the Percent Error
