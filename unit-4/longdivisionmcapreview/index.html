What happens at each stage?

1
9 ÷ 3 = 3

Write the 3 in the quotient above the 9.

2
3 × 3 = 9

Write 9 under the 9, then subtract. There is nothing left, so bring down the 3.

3
3 ÷ 3 = 1

Write 1 in the quotient. Then multiply, subtract, and bring down the 6.

4
6 ÷ 3 = 2

Write 2 in the quotient. Multiply, subtract, and the remainder is 0.

Coach Notes

Quotient on top Every time you divide, write the quotient digit above the bracket.
Subtract rows use a minus sign Each product row sits next to a minus sign because you subtract that amount.
Bring down the next digit After subtracting, the next number appears in the next place-value column.
