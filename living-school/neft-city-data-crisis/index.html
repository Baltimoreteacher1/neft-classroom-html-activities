    🏛️ Mission Briefing

    🧑🏽‍⚖️

    Mayor Reyes

    Welcome to the team! Neft City's new sports arena is amazing — but the concession stands have long wait times. We surveyed 20 visitors and timed how long each one waited in line. Your job: study the data and tell us what the city should do.

    🎯 Your Mission Goals

    • 1 Sort the data from least to greatest.
    • 2 Find the mean, median, mode, and range.
    • 3 Build a histogram (bar graph) of the wait times.
    • 4 Decide what the city should do.
    • 5 Explain your decision using evidence.
