Bridge Diagnostic

24 questions covering computation, fractions, ratios, algebra, geometry, and data. Use before Part 1.

  • Score 0-8: begin with guided small group
  • Score 9-16: assign core pathway
  • Score 17-24: add extension tasks

Part Quizzes

Six 10-point quizzes with 6 fluency items, 3 reasoning items, and 1 written explanation.

  • 8-10: ready to spiral
  • 6-7: reteach one skill
  • 0-5: assign conference and practice set

Final Readiness Task

Students design a mini school fair budget, compare rates, write expressions, graph a layout, and analyze survey data.

  • Math accuracy: 40%
  • Reasoning and explanation: 30%
  • Organization and precision: 20%
  • Reflection: 10%
