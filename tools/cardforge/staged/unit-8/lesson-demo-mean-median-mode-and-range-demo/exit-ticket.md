# Exit Ticket — Mean, Median, Mode, and Range

**Name: _______________________**

1. Find the mean of 6, 10, 8.

2. Find the median of 4, 1, 9, 7, 3.

3. In one sentence, explain the difference between range and mode.

---

### Exit Ticket — Answer Key (teacher)
1. 8 (sum 24 ÷ 3).
2. 4 (ordered 1, 3, 4, 7, 9; middle = 4).
3. Range is greatest minus least (how spread out); mode is the value that appears most often. Accept any correct comparison.
