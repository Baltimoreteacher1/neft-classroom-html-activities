# Template — Student Practice (reference)

Rendered by `lib/generate.mjs → renderStudentPractice(job)`. Student-facing, so
language stays simple and directions are explicit. Sections:

1. Title + name/date line
2. "Today I can:" objective
3. Words to Know (EN/ES vocabulary box with examples)
4. Formulas
5. Worked Examples (the modeled examples, with steps)
6. Directions
7. Practice (numbered prompts, no answers)
8. Explain Your Thinking (reflection/writing prompt)

Answers never appear here — they live in `answer-key.md`.
