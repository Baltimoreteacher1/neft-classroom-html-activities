What Do Good Friends Do?

👂

They Listen

Good friends look at the speaker, stay calm, and listen to understand.

🫶

They Care

Good friends notice feelings and try to help when someone is sad, worried, or left out.

🎉

They Include

Good friends make space for others and do not leave people out on purpose.

🛠️

They Fix Problems

Good friends tell the truth, say sorry when needed, and work things out.
