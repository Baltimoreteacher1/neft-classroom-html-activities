Teacher Guide

Goal Quest Activity Set

Suggested time: 60–75 minutes if students complete most stations; 35–45 minutes if students complete the required 4.

  • Requirement: Students must complete at least 4 stations. The top mission check updates automatically.
  • ESOL access: Each station includes a short reading, icon-based visuals, read-aloud, sentence frames, and optional English/Spanish vocabulary.
  • Student choice: Students choose their path. Encourage one reading station, one decision station, one creation station, and one reflection/planning station.
  • Evidence: Students copy, print, or save the final Goal Passport.
