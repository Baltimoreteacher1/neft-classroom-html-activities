Teacher Guide

Goal Quest: Comprehensive Station Build

Suggested time: 65–90 minutes for a full experience. Students can complete any 4 stations for a shorter class period.

  • Each station includes a short reading, visual mini-comic, vocabulary practice, interactive tasks, and student response space.
  • Students must complete at least 4 stations. The mission tracker at the top checks stations automatically.
  • ESOL supports include icons, short chunks, repeated routines, read-aloud, optional Spanish vocabulary, sentence frames, and choice-based response modes.
  • The final evidence is a Goal Passport students can copy, print, or save as PDF.
