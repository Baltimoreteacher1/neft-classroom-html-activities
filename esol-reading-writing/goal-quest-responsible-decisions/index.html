Teacher Guide

Goal Quest Activity Set

Suggested time: 45–60 minutes. Students can work independently, with partners, or in small groups.

  • Launch: Read the mission and model one simple goal.
  • Choose-your-own-adventure: Let students choose station order. Require all six for a complete passport.
  • ESOL supports: short directions, icons, read-aloud, sentence frames, English/Spanish vocabulary, repeated language.
  • Evidence of learning: final Goal Passport can be copied or printed to PDF.
  • Teacher move: Ask, “Does this choice help the goal?” and “What is your next small step?”

Learning target: I can set a goal and make responsible decisions to help me reach it.
