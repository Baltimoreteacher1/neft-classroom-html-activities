Teacher Guide

Goal Quest: Expanded Station Tabs

Use: 55–75 minutes. Students complete any 4 stations, but the final passport works best after Story + Goal + Choice + Plan/Create.

  • Every tab includes a short reading, visuals, vocabulary/meaning support, and at least one interactive task.
  • Students can choose station order. Completion is checked at the top.
  • ESOL supports include icons, short chunks, repeated sentence frames, read-aloud, and optional Spanish glossary labels.
  • Final evidence: students copy or print the Goal Passport.
