Before you start · Vocabulary

Visual Math Notes

Learn these words first. Tap EN / ES help at the top for Spanish.

📏Dividend

The amount you start with and cut up (the whole board).

Dividendo: la cantidad con la que empiezas y cortas.

🪚Divisor

The size of each piece you cut.

Divisor: el tamaño de cada pieza que cortas.

🧱Quotient

The answer — how many pieces you get.

Cociente: la respuesta — cuántas piezas obtienes.

🔄Reciprocal

A fraction flipped upside down. 1/4 becomes 4/1.

Recíproco: una fracción volteada. 1/4 se vuelve 4/1.

📈Improper Fraction

Top number bigger than bottom, like 7/2.

Fracción impropia: el numerador es mayor que el denominador, como 7/2.

🔢Mixed Number

A whole number with a fraction, like 3 1/2.

Número mixto: un entero con una fracción, como 3 1/2.

Formula Bank

÷ a fraction = × the reciprocal: L ÷ (p/q) = L × (q/p)

Change mixed → improper first: 3 1/2 = (3×2+1)/2 = 7/2

Smaller pieces → more pieces. Cutting smaller pieces gives a bigger answer.

Leftover: full pieces = whole part of the quotient; scrap = remainder × piece size.

📏 Shelf boards (whole number ÷ fraction)

Setup: A board is L feet long. Each shelf is 2/(d) ft. How many full shelves? Formula: L ÷ (2/d) = L × (d/2).

Level 1 tip: try a friendly board like 8 ft with 2/3 ft shelves.

Each shelf is 2 / (denominator) ft
Need a hint?
Dividing by (2/3) is the same as multiplying by (3/2). So 8 ÷ (2/3) = 8 × (3/2) = 24/2 = 12 shelves. Check by multiplying back: 12 × (2/3) = 8 ✅.

Level 1 worked step: flip the divisor 2/3 → 3/2, then 8 × 3 = 24, 24 ÷ 2 = 12.

🎀 Ribbon roll (fraction ÷ fraction)

Setup: You have a/b yards of ribbon. Each piece needs c/d yards. How many pieces? Formula: (a/b) ÷ (c/d) = (a×d)/(b×c).
Ribbon — numerator (a)
Ribbon — denominator (b)
Per piece — numerator (c)
Per piece — denominator (d)
Need a hint?

Multiply by the reciprocal: (5/6) ÷ (1/4) = (5/6) × (4/1) = 20/6 = 10/3 ≈ 3.33 pieces. That is 3 full pieces with 1/3 of a piece left over (1/12 of a yard of ribbon).

🔧 Pipe sections (mixed number ÷ fraction)

Setup: Pipe is W + n/d meters long. Each piece is 1/4 m. Convert the mixed number to an improper fraction, then divide by 1/4.

Need a hint?

Convert 3 1/2 to an improper fraction: (3×2+1)/2 = 7/2 meters. Then divide: (7/2) ÷ (1/4) = (7/2) × (4/1) = 28/2 = 14 pieces. Dividing by 1/4 is the same as multiplying by 4!

Quick division check: A 5-foot rope is cut into 1/2-foot pieces. How many full pieces? (Hint: 5 ÷ 1/2 = ?)

Level 1 worked step: 5 ÷ (1/2) → flip → 5 × 2 = 10.
