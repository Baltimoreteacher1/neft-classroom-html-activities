What is 846 ÷ 3?
Vocabulary: The dividend is the number being divided (846). The divisor is the number you divide by (3). The answer is called the quotient.
Hint: Use the long division algorithm: Divide, Multiply, Subtract, Bring down.
✓Correct! 846 ÷ 3 = 282. Divide each place: 8÷3 = 2 R2, bring down 4 to get 24, 24÷3 = 8, bring down 6, 6÷3 = 2.
✗Not quite. Use long division step by step: 8÷3 = 2 R2, 24÷3 = 8, 6÷3 = 2. The quotient is B: 282.
Warm-Up 2
What is 1,575 ÷ 5?
Hint: Set up the long division. 5 goes into 15 three times, then bring down and continue.
✓Correct! 1,575 ÷ 5 = 315. Step by step: 15÷5 = 3, 7÷5 = 1 R2, 25÷5 = 5.
✗Try again: 15÷5 = 3, bring down 7, 7÷5 = 1 R2, bring down 5 to get 25, 25÷5 = 5. The answer is C: 315.
