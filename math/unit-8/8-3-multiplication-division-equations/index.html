Which equation represents the phrase: "a number multiplied by 4 equals 28"?
Vocabulary: A coefficient is the number multiplied by the variable. In 4n, the coefficient is 4.
Hint: "A number" means a variable. "Multiplied by 4" means 4 times that variable. "Equals 28" tells you the right side.
✓Correct! "A number multiplied by 4" translates to 4n, and "equals 28" gives us 4n = 28.
✗Not quite. "Multiplied by 4" means multiplication, so the equation is B: 4n = 28.
Warm-Up 2
True or false: To solve 5x = 35, you should divide both sides by 5.
Vocabulary: An inverse operation "undoes" another operation. Division undoes multiplication, and multiplication undoes division.
Hint: Since x is being multiplied by 5, ask yourself: what undoes multiplication?
✓Correct! Division is the inverse operation of multiplication. Dividing both sides by 5 gives x = 35 / 5 = 7.
✗Actually, this is true. Since x is multiplied by 5, you divide both sides by 5 to isolate x. That gives x = 7.
