Reveal Math · Unit 8

Equations & Inequalities

Write, solve, and understand one-step equations and inequalities to find unknown values and describe real-world situations. Standard 6.EE.

Lesson 8.1 — Understand Equations & Solutions

What an equation is, what a solution is, checking if an answer is correct. Foundation for all future algebra.

Lesson 8.2 — Solve Equations (Addition & Subtraction)

Write equations from word problems, solve x + 5 = 12 and x − 7 = 9 using inverse operations.

Lesson 8.3 — Solve Equations (Multiplication & Division)

Solve 4x = 20 and x ÷ 6 = 3, use inverse operations to undo math.

Lesson 8.4 — Write & Represent Inequalities

Inequality symbols (>, <, ≥, ≤), represent inequalities on a number line.

Lesson 8.5 — Understand Inequality Solutions

Solve inequalities, understand that inequalities have many solutions, check using reasoning.
