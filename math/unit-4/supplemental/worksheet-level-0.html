Decimals & Percents

Grade 6 · Standards 6.NS.B.2, 6.NS.B.3, 6.RP.A.3c — decimal operations & percents

Name: Date:

Word Bank

🔘Decimal — a number with a point that shows part of a whole (3.75).
📍Place value — tenths, hundredths, thousandths after the point.
％Percent — "out of 100." 25% means 25 per 100.
🟰Equivalent forms — the same value as a fraction, decimal, or percent.
➗Long division — dividing step by step to get a decimal answer.
🧮Percent of a number — multiply the number by the percent as a decimal.

Worked Example

Find 25% of 80

Step 1: Write the percent as a decimal: 25% = 0.25.
Step 2: Multiply: 0.25 × 80.
Step 3: Calculate: 0.25 × 80 = 20.
Answer: 25% of 80 is 20.

Guided Practice

Directions: Solve each problem. Show your work and use the hint cue when you need help.
  1. Add: 3.4 + 2.75 = ______
    Hint: line up the decimal points; add a zero so 3.40.
  2. Subtract: 6.5 − 1.82 = ______
    Hint: line up the points; write 6.50, then subtract.
  3. Multiply: 0.6 × 0.4 = ______
    Hint: multiply 6 × 4 = 24, then count 2 decimal places.
  4. Divide: 4.5 ÷ 5 = ______
    Hint: 45 ÷ 5 = 9, then place the decimal point.
  5. Write 0.75 as a percent. ______
    Hint: move the point two places right (×100).
  6. Write 40% as a decimal. ______
    Hint: move the point two places left (÷100).
  7. Write 1/2 as a decimal and a percent. ______
    Hint: 1 ÷ 2 = 0.5, then ×100 for the percent.
  8. Find 10% of 250. ______
    Hint: 10% is one-tenth — divide by 10.
  9. Find 50% of 64. ______
    Hint: 50% is half.
  10. A shirt costs $20. It is 25% off. How much is the discount in dollars? ______
    Hint: find 25% of 20 first.

Answer Key

  1. 3.40 + 2.75 = 6.15.
  2. 6.50 − 1.82 = 4.68.
  3. 0.6 × 0.4 = 0.24.
  4. 4.5 ÷ 5 = 0.9.
  5. 0.75 = 75%.
  6. 40% = 0.40.
  7. 1/2 = 0.5 = 50%.
  8. 250 ÷ 10 = 25.
  9. 64 ÷ 2 = 32.
  10. 25% of $20 = $5 discount.
