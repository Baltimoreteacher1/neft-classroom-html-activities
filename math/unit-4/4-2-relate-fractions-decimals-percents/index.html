True or false: 0.25 and 25% represent the same value.
Hint: To convert a decimal to a percent, multiply by 100. 0.25 × 100 = 25. So 0.25 = 25%.
✓Correct! 0.25 = 25/100 = 25%. They are equivalent.
✗0.25 × 100 = 25, so 0.25 = 25%. The statement is true.
