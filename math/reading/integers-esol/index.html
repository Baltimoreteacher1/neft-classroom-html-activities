Captain Mia's Submarine

Captain Mia dives below the sea. Integers, absolute value, and comparing numbers describe depth, height, and distance.

ESOL Reading + Math Integers & Absolute Value 6.NS.5–7 Levels: Beginner & Intermediate
Before You Read

Key Words

integer — a whole number that can be positive, negative, or zero: …−2, −1, 0, 1, 2…
negative number — a number less than zero, like −20 (below sea level).
absolute value — the distance from zero, always positive. |−20| = 20.
opposite — the same distance from 0 on the other side. The opposite of −8 is 8.
compare — decide which is greater. On a number line, left is less.
sea level — zero. Above is positive; below is negative.
Part 1 — Below Sea Level (Integers)

Captain Mia's submarine dove to 20 feet below sea level. On a number line, sea level is 0, and below is negative.

20 feet below sea level is written as the integer −20.

Check Your Reading — Q1

Why is the depth written as a negative integer?

Solve It — #1

Write "15 feet below sea level" as an integer.

Part 2 — Distance from the Surface (Absolute Value)

"How far are we from the surface?" asked the crew. Distance is always positive — that is the absolute value. The sub was at −45 feet.

|−45| = 45 feet from the surface. (Absolute value ignores the sign; it is just the distance from 0.)

Check Your Reading — Q2

What does absolute value measure?

Solve It — #2

Find |−18| (the distance of −18 from zero).

Part 3 — Which Is Deeper? (Comparing)

Two subs reported depths: −3 and −7. "Which is deeper?" On a number line, the number farther left is less, so −7 is less than −3 — and deeper.

−7 < −3. The more negative number is lower (deeper).

Solve It — #3

Order these from least to greatest and type the least: −5, 2, −1.

Part 4 — Opposites

The sub at −8 feet rose to its opposite position: +8 feet (above the surface, like a periscope tower). Opposites are the same distance from 0 but on different sides.

The opposite of −8 is 8. The opposite of 8 is −8.

Solve It — #4

What is the opposite of 6?
