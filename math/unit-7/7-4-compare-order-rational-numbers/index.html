Warm-Up 2
True or false: −2 > −1
Vocabulary: The symbol > means "is greater than." A number is greater if it is farther right on the number line.
Think: Is −2 to the right of −1?
✓Correct! −2 is to the left of −1, so −2 < −1. The statement −2 > −1 is false.
✗−2 is to the left of −1 on the number line, which means −2 is less than −1. The statement is false.
