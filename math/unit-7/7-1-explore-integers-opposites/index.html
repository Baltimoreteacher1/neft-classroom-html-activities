Warm-Up 1
What integer represents "a loss of 15 yards"?
Vocabulary: An integer is any whole number, including positive numbers, negative numbers, and zero. A loss or decrease is represented by a negative number.
✓Correct! A loss is negative, so "a loss of 15 yards" is represented by the integer -15.
✗Not quite. A "loss" means a decrease, which is a negative number. The answer is B: -15.
Warm-Up 2
True or False: The opposite of -7 is 7.
Vocabulary: The opposite of a number is the same distance from 0 on the number line, but on the other side. For example, the opposite of 3 is -3.
✓Correct! -7 and 7 are on opposite sides of 0, each 7 units away. They are opposites.
✗Actually, the opposite of -7 is 7. Both are 7 units from 0 but on opposite sides of the number line.
