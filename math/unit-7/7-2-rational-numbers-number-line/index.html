Warm-Up 2
True or False: The opposite of 3/4 is -3/4.
Vocabulary: The opposite of a number is the same distance from zero but on the other side of the number line. For example, the opposite of 5 is -5.
✓Correct! The opposite of 3/4 is -3/4. Both are the same distance (3/4 of a unit) from zero, but on opposite sides.
✗Actually, this is true. The opposite of any number has the same absolute value but the opposite sign. The opposite of 3/4 is -3/4.
