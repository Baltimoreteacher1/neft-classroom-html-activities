Warm-Up 1
A car travels 120 miles in 2 hours. Which of the following is a rate?
Vocabulary: A rate is a ratio that compares two quantities with different units (like miles and hours).
✓Correct! "120 miles per 2 hours" compares miles to hours — two different units — making it a rate.
✗A rate compares two different units. "120 miles per 2 hours" compares miles to hours. The answer is B.
Warm-Up 2
If you earn $24 for 3 hours of work, what is your unit rate (dollars per hour)?
Hint: A unit rate has a denominator of 1. Divide $24 ÷ 3 hours to find the pay per one hour.
✓Correct! $24 ÷ 3 = $8 per hour.
✗Divide total dollars by total hours: $24 ÷ 3 = $8. The answer is B: $8 per hour.
