Warm-Up 1
Which pair of ratios is equivalent?
Vocabulary: Equivalent ratios are ratios that name the same comparison. You can multiply or divide both parts of a ratio by the same number.
✓Correct! 2:3 and 4:6 are equivalent because 2×2=4 and 3×2=6.
✗Multiply both parts of 2:3 by 2: 2×2=4, 3×2=6 → 4:6. The answer is B.

Practice 3
Are the ratios 6:9 and 10:15 equivalent?
Strategy: Simplify both ratios by dividing by their GCF. If they simplify to the same ratio, they are equivalent.
Show your simplification:

Sentence frame: "6:9 simplifies to ___:___ and 10:15 simplifies to ___:___. They are ___."

✓Correct! 6:9 = 2:3 and 10:15 = 2:3. They are equivalent.
✗Simplify: 6÷3=2, 9÷3=3 → 2:3. And 10÷5=2, 15÷5=3 → 2:3. They are equivalent.
