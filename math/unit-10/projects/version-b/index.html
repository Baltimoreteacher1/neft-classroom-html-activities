Before you start · Vocabulary

Visual Math Notes

Learn these words first. Tap EN / ES help at the top for Spanish.

🧊Volume

The amount of space inside a 3-D shape, in cubic units.

Volumen: el espacio dentro de una figura 3-D, en unidades cúbicas.

📐Surface Area

The total area of all the outside faces, in square units.

Área de superficie: el área total de todas las caras exteriores, en unidades cuadradas.

🗺️Net

A flat pattern that folds up into a 3-D solid.

Plantilla (net): un patrón plano que se dobla para formar un sólido 3-D.

📦Rectangular Prism

A box shape with 6 rectangular faces (an open-top tank shows 5).

Prisma rectangular: una caja con 6 caras (un tanque abierto muestra 5).

📏Edge Length

How long one side of the tank is (l, w, or h).

Longitud de arista: lo que mide un lado del tanque (l, w o h).

⬜Unit Cube

A cube 1 unit on every side; volume = 1 cubic unit of water.

Cubo unitario: un cubo de 1 unidad por lado; volumen = 1 unidad cúbica de agua.

Formula Bank

Full volume: V = l × w × h

Water volume: Vwater = l × w × fill height

Open-top SA (no lid): SA = lw + 2lh + 2wh

Closed-box SA (for comparison): SA = 2(lw + lh + wh)

Glass cost: cost = SA × cost per square unit

Efficiency: glass per cube = SA ÷ V (smaller is better)

💡 Guided Steps & Help

Prism Volume & Surface Area from 3D Nets:

  1. **Volume ($V$) of a rectangular prism**: $V = l \times w \times h$. Multiply length, width, and height. If fractional, convert to improper fractions first.
  2. **Surface Area ($SA$)**: Sum of the areas of all 6 rectangular faces in the unfolded net:
    • Calculate Area = $2(lw + lh + wh)$.
  3. Be sure to match the units: volume is cubic units ($u^3$) and surface area is square units ($u^2$).
💡 Example: Prism with $l=5, w=3, h=2$. Volume = $5 \times 3 \times 2 = \mathbf{30}$ cubic units. Surface Area = $2(15 + 10 + 6) = 2(31) = \mathbf{62}$ sq units.

🔷 Glass needed — open-top SA = lw + 2lh + 2wh

A fish tank is open at the top — no glass lid. Uses your tank dimensions above.

Check my work: Compute the open-top glass area (5 faces, no top), then type it.
Need a hint?

Closed box: SA = 2(lw + lh + wh). Open-top removes the top face (one l × w panel), so SA = lw + 2lh + 2wh. For 5 × 2 × 3: 10 + 2(15) + 2(6) = 10 + 30 + 12 = 52 sq units.
