Warm-Up 2
A survey shows that 60% of students prefer pizza for lunch. If there are 40 students, how many prefer pizza?
Hint: To find a percent of a number, convert the percent to a decimal and multiply. 60% = 0.60.
✓Correct! 60% of 40 = 0.60 × 40 = 24 students.
✗Convert 60% to 0.60, then multiply: 0.60 × 40 = 24 students (B).
