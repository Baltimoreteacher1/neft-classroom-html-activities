Mission 0: Launch Code

Before using AI, set the rules. Gemini is your coach — you are the mathematician.

5 min
🎯

AI Skill Unlocked: The Setup Prompt

Tell AI exactly what role to play and what rules to follow — before you ask anything else.

AI Adventure Rules

  1. Ask for coaching first. Try: "Do not give me the answer yet."
  2. Ask clarifying questions. Try: "What should I do first?"
  3. Show your work. Gemini can help check, but your math must make sense.
  4. Verify everything. AI can make mistakes — always double-check.
  5. No private info. Never type names, addresses, or personal details.

Ask AI to guide you step-by-step instead of giving the answer. Try: "Coach me through this — don't give the final answer yet."

MedianThe middle value when numbers are sorted from least to greatest. If there are two middle numbers, find the average of both.La mediana: el valor del medio en datos ordenados. Reminder:

Put the numbers in order from least to greatest. If there is one middle number, that is the median. If there are two middle numbers, find the number halfway between them (add them and divide by 2).

Mission 2: Mode Marsh

The marsh has repeating clues. Tap each number to count it, then find the value that appears most often.

🔍

AI Skill Unlocked: The Verification Loop

After you solve a problem, ask AI to verify your reasoning step-by-step — don't just ask "is this right?"

ModeThe number that appears most often in a data set. A set can have one mode, more than one mode, or no mode at all.La moda: el número que aparece con más frecuencia en un conjunto de datos. Reminder:

The mode is the number that appears most often. A data set can have one mode, more than one mode (if there's a tie for the most frequent), or no mode (if every number appears the same number of times).

Compare Prompt Levels

❌ Weak Prompt

"What's the answer?"

Problem: Skips your thinking entirely. You learn nothing.

🔶 Better Prompt

"Coach me through the first step. Don't give the final answer yet."

Better: Protects your learning by slowing AI down.

✅ Best Prompt

"Here is my work: [paste work]. Check it, ask one clarifying question, and explain any mistake with a hint."

Best: Uses YOUR work as evidence. AI verifies instead of solving.
