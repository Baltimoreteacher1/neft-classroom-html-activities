Mission 1: The Balanced Spread
Create a dataset with **at least 8 dots** where the Box Plot is **perfectly symmetrical** (distance from Min to Q1 equals Q3 to Max, and Q1 to Median equals Median to Q3).
✓
Mission 2: Left-Skewed Shift
Position your dots to build a **skewed-left distribution** where the **Median is greater than the Mean** by at least **0.5 units** (Requires at least 6 data points).
✓
Mission 3: The Target IQR
Build a dataset with **at least 10 data points** where the Interquartile Range (IQR) is **exactly 4 units**.
