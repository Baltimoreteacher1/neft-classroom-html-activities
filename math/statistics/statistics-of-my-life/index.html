Visual Math Notes

Use these words to describe the story in a data set.

⚖️Mean

Add all numbers. Divide by how many numbers.

Promedio: suma y divide por la cantidad.

🎯Median

Put numbers in order. Find the middle.

Mediana: el número del medio.

🔁Mode

The number that repeats the most.

Moda: el número que más se repite.

↔️Range

Greatest number minus smallest number.

Rango: mayor menos menor.

Formula Bank

Mean: sum ÷ count

Median: order → middle

Mode: most frequent

Range: max − min

Sentence Frames

“The data shows ___ because ___.”

“My mean is higher/lower than ___, so ___.”

“The range is ___, which means the data is ___.”
