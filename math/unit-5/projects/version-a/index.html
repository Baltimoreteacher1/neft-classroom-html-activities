Before you start · Vocabulary

Visual Math Notes

Learn these words first. Tap EN / ES help at the top for Spanish.

📏Base (b)

One full side of a shape that you measure along.

Base: un lado completo de la figura que mides.

📐Height (h) — perpendicular!

The straight-up distance from the base, making a right angle — NOT the slanted side.

Altura: la distancia recta (perpendicular) desde la base, no el lado inclinado.

▱Parallelogram

A 4-sided shape with both pairs of sides parallel. Area = b × h.

Paralelogramo: figura de 4 lados con lados paralelos. Área = b × h.

🔺Triangle

Exactly half a parallelogram. Area = ½ × b × h.

Triángulo: la mitad de un paralelogramo. Área = ½ × b × h.

⏢Trapezoid

A 4-sided shape with two parallel bases. Area = ½(b₁+b₂)h.

Trapecio: figura con dos bases paralelas. Área = ½(b₁+b₂)h.

🧩Composite figure

A shape made of simpler shapes. Decompose, find each, then add.

Figura compuesta: hecha de figuras simples. Descompón, halla cada una y suma.

🟦Square unit

How area is measured — like square feet (sq ft) or square tiles.

Unidad cuadrada: cómo se mide el área, como pies cuadrados.

Formula Bank

Parallelogram / rectangle: Area = b × h

Triangle: Area = ½ × b × h

Trapezoid: Area = ½ × (b₁ + b₂) × h

Composite: decompose into parts, find each, then add

With a hole: Area = (whole rectangle) − (cut-out shape)

Missing dimension: b = Area ÷ h  |  h = Area ÷ b

Sentence Frames

"The area of my ___ is ___ sq ft because I used Area = ___."

"My total room area is ___ sq ft, the sum of ___ parts."

"My room is bigger / smaller than ___ by ___ sq ft."

"El área de mi ___ es ___ pies² porque usé Área = ___."

Need a hint?

Multiply base × height. The height must be perpendicular (straight up, a right angle) to the base — not the slanted side. Example: b = 12, h = 10 → Area = 12 × 10 = 120 sq ft.

Part 1b · Self — your real plan

🔺 Reading nook (triangle)

Area = ½ × b × h
Need a hint?

A triangle is half a parallelogram: Area = ½ × b × h. Example: b = 6, h = 5 → Area = ½ × 6 × 5 = 15 sq ft. Don't forget the ½!

Part 1c · Self — your real plan

🧩 L-shaped bonus area (composite)

Composite Area = Area A + Area B | each part: b × h

Split the L into two rectangles, find each, then add.

Need a hint?

Area A = b × h, Area B = b × h, then add. Example: A = 10 × 8 = 80, B = 6 × 5 = 30, total = 110 sq ft.
