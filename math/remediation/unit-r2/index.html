🍕 Fraction Foundations

Five step-by-step lessons that take you from understanding what a fraction means all the way through multiplying fractions and mixed numbers. Every skill here unlocks the fraction division you'll need in Grade 6 (6.NS.1). Each lesson follows: Learn It → Try It → Practice → Exit Ticket.

For the Teacher

Using Unit R2

Who it's for: Grade 7–8 students who struggle with fraction concepts before tackling Grade 6 fraction division (6.NS.1) or any work with rational numbers.

Suggested sequence: Lessons 1 → 5 build on each other. Students who are solid on equivalent fractions (L2) can skip L1; students weak on comparing (L3) should complete L2 first. All lessons end with a self-checking exit ticket.

Fraction visuals: Every lesson uses inline SVG fraction bars so students can see the math, not just compute it. No images, no external files needed.
