Bridge to Grade 6

A foundations-first remediation track for Grade 7–8 students who need to rebuild the essential skills that Grade 6 math is built on. Five short units, three interactive lessons each, every lesson tagged with the Grade 6 standard it unlocks. Each lesson follows the same path: Learn It → Try It → Practice → Exit Ticket.

How It Works

Start Where You Need To

Each lesson is self-paced and self-checking, with worked examples, guided practice with instant feedback, independent practice, and a short exit ticket. No login, no setup — open and learn.

For the Teacher

Using the Bridge

Who it's for: Grade 7–8 students who are working to close gaps and get ready for (or catch up within) Grade 6 math. Skills here are the upstream prerequisites for the Grade 6 standards.

How to assign: use a diagnostic to send students to the right unit, or move through R1 → R5 in order. Each lesson ends with a self-checking exit ticket, and lessons connect explicitly to the Grade 6 standard they unlock so students see the payoff.

Pairs with: the Manipulatives Toolkit for hands-on modeling and the Bilingual Vocab Deck for ESOL support.
