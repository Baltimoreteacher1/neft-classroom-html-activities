For the Teacher

Using Unit R4

Standard unlocked: 6.NS.4 — find the GCF and LCM of two whole numbers and apply the distributive property. Unit R4 builds every prerequisite for that standard from the ground up.

Suggested sequence: Lessons 1 → 2 are essential prerequisites; complete them before Lessons 3 and 4. Lesson 5 stands alone and can be used as enrichment or a bridge to Unit 6 expressions work.

Pairs with: the Manipulatives Toolkit for hands-on factor tiles and number lines, and the Bilingual Vocab Deck for factor / multiple / prime / composite vocabulary support.
