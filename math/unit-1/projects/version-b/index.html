Before you start · Vocabulary

Visual Math Notes

Learn these words first. Tap EN / ES help at the top for Spanish.

✖️Decimal Multiplication

Price × quantity, keeping the decimal places.

Multiplicación de decimales: precio × cantidad, manteniendo los decimales.

➗Multi-Digit Division

Splitting a cost into equal shares.

División de varios dígitos: dividir un costo en partes iguales.

🧰GCF — Greatest Common Factor

The biggest number that divides two counts evenly.

MCD — Máximo Común Divisor: el número más grande que divide a ambos sin sobrar.

⏱️LCM — Least Common Multiple

The first number two cycles share.

mcm — mínimo común múltiplo: el primer número que comparten dos ciclos.

Formula Bank

Line total: unit price × quantity

Fair share: total cost ÷ team members

GCF(a, b): biggest shared factor → number of equal kits

Budget left: budget − total spent

Distributive (6.NS.4): a + b = GCF × (a÷GCF + b÷GCF)

Sentence Frames

"My parts subtotal is $___ because price × quantity = ___."

"Each teammate pays $___ because total ÷ members = ___."

"The GCF of ___ and ___ is ___, so I can build ___ kits."

"Mi subtotal de piezas es $___ porque precio × cantidad = ___."
