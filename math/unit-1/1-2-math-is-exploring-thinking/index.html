Strategy: Add 1/3 + 1/4. The common denominator of 3 and 4 is 12. So 1/3 = 4/12 and 1/4 = 3/12. Now add.
Show your work:

Sentence frame: "1/3 = ___/12 and 1/4 = ___/12. Together they ate ___/12 of the pizza."

✓Correct! 1/3 = 4/12 and 1/4 = 3/12. So 4/12 + 3/12 = 7/12 of the pizza. Great exploring!
✗Use 12 as the common denominator: 1/3 = 4/12, 1/4 = 3/12. The total is 4/12 + 3/12 = 7/12 (B).
