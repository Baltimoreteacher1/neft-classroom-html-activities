D. Suggested Form Questions

Class Check-In

  • How is math class going for you right now?
  • What is helping you learn?
  • What is making class hard?
  • What should Mr. Neft know?
  • Do you want Mr. Neft to follow up with you?

I'm Confused

  • What lesson, topic, direction, or assignment is confusing?
  • What part did you understand?
  • Where did you get stuck?
  • What would help you next?
  • Do you want an example, small group, video, or partner help?

Suggestion Box

  • What is your idea?
  • What problem would this solve?
  • How would it help students?
  • Is this idea for lessons, games, groups, homework, classroom routines, or something else?

Shout-Out

  • Who or what do you want to shout out?
  • What happened?
  • Why was it positive?
  • Should this be shared with the class?

Private Note

  • What do you want Mr. Neft to know?
  • Is this about class, work, a classmate, group work, homework, or something else?
  • Do you need Mr. Neft to follow up?
  • Is this urgent?

Anonymous Question

  • What question do you want to ask?
  • Is this about math, directions, grades, homework, class routines, or something else?
  • Would it help if Mr. Neft answered this for the whole class?

E. Weekly Review Routine

  • MondayScan check-ins.

  • Any dayImmediately act on safety concerns.
