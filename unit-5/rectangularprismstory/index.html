Case notes
Find surface area by drawing or imagining the net, finding each rectangular face area, pairing opposite faces, then adding all six faces.
Exit ticket
How is surface area different from volume?
Net

A flat pattern of all faces of a 3D figure.

Face Area

For each rectangular face, multiply the two side lengths.

Surface Area

Add the areas of all outside faces. Units are square units.
