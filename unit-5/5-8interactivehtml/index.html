Surface Area of Square Pyramids

Find the outside area by adding the square base and the four triangle faces.

Practice progress0 / 10
Keep this visible
SA = b² + 4(½bs)
b = base side   |   s = slant height
1Base: b × b
2Triangles: 4 × (½ × b × s)
3Total: base + triangles
Slant height is the height of one triangle face.

Today’s Goal

Use a simple formula to find the total surface area of a square pyramid.

What you will do

1Find the square base area.
2Find the area of all 4 triangles.
3Add them together.

Learn the Parts

Only two measurements are needed: the side of the square base and the slant height of a triangle face.

Vocabulary

Surface AreaTotal outside area.
BaseThe bottom square.
FaceOne side triangle.
Slant HeightHeight of a triangle face.
Use the triangle’s slant height, not the straight height inside the pyramid.
