1. Learn the Big Idea

Volume measures the amount of space inside a 3D figure. A rectangular prism has three dimensions: length, width, and height.

Formula Anchor

How to Solve

  1. 1Identify the dimensions. Find length, width, and height.
  2. 2Write the formula. Use V = l × w × h.
  3. 3Multiply or divide. To find volume, multiply. To find a missing side, divide the volume by the product of the two known dimensions.
  4. 4Label the answer. Volume uses cubic units, such as cm³. A missing side uses regular units, such as cm.

Vocabulary Support

Word Student-Friendly Meaning Sentence Frame
volume The amount of space inside a 3D shape. The volume is ___ cubic units.
rectangular prism A box-shaped solid with rectangular faces. The prism has dimensions ___, ___, and ___.
unit cube A cube used to measure volume. There are ___ unit cubes in the prism.
missing side A length, width, or height that is not shown. I can divide the volume by ___ to find the missing side.
