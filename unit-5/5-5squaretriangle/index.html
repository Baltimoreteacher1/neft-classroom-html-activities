Square Pyramid Attributes

A square pyramid has one square base and four triangular side faces that meet at one vertex.

PartHow many?Student note
Square base1bottom polygon
Triangular faces4one on each side of the square
Total faces54 triangles + 1 square
Edges8where faces meet
Vertices5corners, including top vertex
Say it: “The pyramid has four triangular faces and one square base.”

⏱ 8–10 minutes

Summarize: Nets Represent 3D Figures

A net is a two-dimensional representation of a three-dimensional figure. When you unfold a 3D figure, you see its 2D faces.
