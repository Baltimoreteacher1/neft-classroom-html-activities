Detective Case Files: The Equation Mysteries

You are Detective Mateo. 🕵️ Each clue hides an unknown number. You write an equation and then solve it to open the locked evidence boxes. Crack the case!

Eres el Detective Mateo. Cada pista esconde un número desconocido. Escribe una ecuación y resuélvela para abrir las cajas.

Act 1 · Write Equations

The First Clue

🕵️ Clue #1 — Write the Equation

The note says: “The thief took 7 coins from the jar. Now 12 coins are left.” Let c = how many coins were there at the start. Which equation matches the clue? El ladrón se llevó 7 monedas. Quedan 12. ¿Cuál ecuación es correcta?

💡 Hint: Start amount, take away 7, equals 12 left. “Take away” means subtract: c − 7.

🕵️ Clue #2 — Write the Equation

New clue: “3 equal bags of gems hold 24 gems in all.” Let g = the number of gems in one bag. Which equation matches? 3 bolsas iguales tienen 24 gemas. ¿Cuál ecuación es correcta?

💡 Hint: 3 equal bags means 3 groups of g. Equal groups means multiply: 3 × g, written 3g.
Sentence frame: “The unknown is the letter ____. The words ‘took away’ mean ____. The words ‘equal bags’ mean ____. My equation is ____.”
🔒 Finish Act 1 first to unlock Act 2.
Act 2 · Solve Equations

The Locked Boxes

🔐 Box #1 — Solve to Unlock

The first box code is the value of x. Solve the equation to get the code. Resuelve para hallar el código x.

x + 8 = 21
💡 Hint: To undo + 8, do the opposite: subtract 8 from both sides. 21 − 8 = ?

🔐 Box #2 — Solve to Unlock

The second box code is the value of n. Solve to get the code. Resuelve para hallar n.

5n = 45
💡 Hint: 5n means 5 times n. To undo × 5, do the opposite: divide by 5. 45 ÷ 5 = ?
Sentence frame: “To undo adding 8, I ____ 8. So x = ____. To undo multiplying by 5, I ____ by 5. So n = ____.”
🔒 Finish Act 2 first to unlock the Final Case.
Final Case · Boss

The Vault Mystery

🔒 The Vault — Write AND Solve

The final clue: “A secret number plus 9 equals 23.” Let the number be v. First write the equation, then solve it. Pick the line that is fully correct. Un número más 9 es 23. Escribe la ecuación y resuélvela.

💡 Hint: “plus 9” means v + 9. “equals 23” means = 23. To solve, subtract 9: 23 − 9 = ?
Sentence frame: “The equation is v + 9 = 23. To solve I ____ 9 from both sides. The number is v = ____.”
