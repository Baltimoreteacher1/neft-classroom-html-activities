Global Travel Planner

Maya has won a contest: plan one perfect around-the-world expedition. But every destination is sealed behind a ratio cipher. Paris: Reason with Ratios

🇫🇷 Cipher #1 — Equivalent Ratios

🇫🇷 Cipher #2 — Unit Rate

The shuttle covers 18 km using 3 liters of fuel. To compare offers, Maya needs the unit rate: kilometers per 1 liter. What is it?
