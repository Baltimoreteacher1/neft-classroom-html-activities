Shopping Mall Mogul 🛍️

Mateo just took over the Nexus Galleria, the city’s most ambitious smart-mall. Every shop he wants to open is gated behind a pricing puzzle. To out-negotiate rival retailers and pack the galleria, he must master unit rates, percent markdowns, and the slippery world of percents over 100% and under 1%. Sharpen your deal-making instincts — the best bargain hunter wins the mall.

Act 1 · Rates & Unit Rates

The Best Buy

🛒 Supplier Bid #1 — Best Value

Two suppliers pitch the same energy drink. Vendor A: a 750 mL bottle for $4.50. Vendor B: a 1.25 L (1250 mL) bottle for $6.25. Stock the better value — compare the price per millilitre.

🛒 Supplier Bid #2 — Price per 100 g

The roastery offers 240 g of premium coffee for $9.60. Shoppers compare prices per 100 g. What is the unit rate per 100 g?

⭐ Mogul’s Bonus

Optional extra credit — you can open the shop without it.

The Loyalty Reorder

A boutique reorders notebooks. Pack X: 5 for $7.50. Pack Y: 8 for $11.20. Which is the better buy, and by how much per notebook?

🔒 Complete Act 1 to unlock Act 2.
Act 2 · Fractions, Decimals & Percents

The Markdown War

🏷️ Markdown #1 — The Sale Price

A rival drops sneaker prices, so you counter. Your sneakers list at $80 and you mark them 35% off. What is the sale price shoppers pay?

🏷️ Markdown #2 — Beyond 100% and Below 1%

Your dashboard shows growth of 250% and a tiny transaction-fee of 0.5%. Percents can be greater than 100% or less than 1%. Which row converts both correctly?

⭐ Mogul’s Bonus

Optional extra credit — skip it and still launch the sale.

Reverse the Markdown

A designer coat is on the rack at $60 after a 25% off sale. Working backward, what was the original price?

🔒 Complete Act 2 to unlock the Grand Opening.
Grand Opening · Boss

The Headline Deal

⭐ Headline Deal — Both Skills

The grand-opening special: a 6-pack of smart bulbs at $48, then 15% off. Find the final price for one bulb.

⭐ Mogul’s Final Bonus

Optional extra credit — the mall opens whether or not you solve it.

The Smarter Bundle

A competitor counters your bulb deal. Deal A (yours): 6 for $48, then 15% off. Deal B (theirs): 4 for $30, then 10% off. Which deal is the better value per bulb?

🎉🛍️⭐

Galleria Launched — Mission Complete!

The Nexus Galleria is the busiest mall in the city. You out-priced every rival with sharp unit-rate comparisons, ran precise percent markdowns, and even handled percents beyond 100% and below 1%. Investors are calling, Mogul — the empire is just getting started.
