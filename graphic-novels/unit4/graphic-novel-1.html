Shopping Mall Mogul 🛍️

You are Mateo. You run a new shopping mall! To win customers and open shops, you must find the best deals. Use unit rates and percent discounts to be the best mall mogul in the city!

Eres Mateo. Diriges un centro comercial. Usa las matemáticas para encontrar las mejores ofertas.

Act 1 · Unit Rates

The Best Buy

🛒 Deal Check #1 — Best Buy

The Game Shop sells apps two ways. To find the best buy, find the unit rate (price for ONE app): price ÷ number of apps. Which pack is cheaper per app? Encuentra el precio por UNA app (precio ÷ cantidad). ¿Cuál es más barato?

💡 Hint: 3 apps for $6 → $6 ÷ 3. 5 apps for $8 → $8 ÷ 5. The smaller answer per app is the better buy.

🛒 Deal Check #2 — Price per Ounce

The Smoothie Bar sells a 12 oz cup for $3. What is the unit rate (price for ONE ounce)? ¿Cuál es el precio por UNA onza? ($3 ÷ 12 oz)

💡 Hint: Unit rate = price ÷ quantity = $3 ÷ 12 oz.
Sentence frame: “The unit rate is ____ per ____. The best buy is ____ because it costs ____ for one item.”
🔒 Finish Act 1 first to unlock Act 2.
Act 2 · Percents

The Big Sale

🏷️ Sale Tag #1 — Percent Off

A jacket costs $40. Today it is 25% off. How much money do you save (the discount)? ¿Cuánto dinero ahorras con 25% de descuento de $40?

💡 Hint: 25% = 0.25. Discount = $40 × 0.25. (Or: 25% is ¼ of $40.)

🏷️ Sale Tag #2 — Same Value

The sale sign shows 0.75 of the price stays. A fraction, decimal, and percent can all show the SAME value. Which group all equals 0.75? ¿Cuál grupo es igual a 0.75 (fracción, decimal y porcentaje)?

💡 Hint: To make a decimal a percent, move the dot 2 places: 0.75 = 75%. And 75% = 75/100 = 3/4.
Sentence frame: “25% of $40 is ____. The decimal 0.75 is the same as ____% and the fraction ____.”
🔒 Finish Act 2 first to unlock the Grand Opening.
Grand Opening · Boss

The Best Deal of All

⭐ Grand Opening Deal — Both Skills

A 4-pack of phone chargers costs $20. First find the unit rate (one charger). Then take 20% off that price. What is the final price for ONE charger? Encuentra el precio de UN cargador, luego 20% de descuento.

💡 Hint: Step 1: $20 ÷ 4 = $5 each. Step 2: 20% off $5 → $5 × 0.20 = $1 off → $5 − $1.
Sentence frame: “One charger is $20 ÷ 4 = ____. 20% off is ____ saved, so the final price is ____.”
