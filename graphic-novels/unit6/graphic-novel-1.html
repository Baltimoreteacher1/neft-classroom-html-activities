Festival Producer: Power Up the Show 🎤

You are Mika, a young music festival producer. The big show is tonight! But the stages are dark. To turn on the speakers, lights, and screens, you must solve powers and expressions. Power up the show!

Eres Mika, productora de un festival de música. Usa potencias y expresiones para encender el escenario.

Act 1 · Powers & Exponents

Speaker Power

🔊 Speaker Lock #1

The speaker power is 24. A power means repeated multiplication. The base is 2 and the exponent is 4. What is 24? ¿Cuánto es 24 (2 multiplicado 4 veces)?

💡 Hint: 24 = 2 × 2 × 2 × 2. Multiply two at a time: 2×2 = 4, then 4×2 = 8, then 8×2 = 16.

💡 Light Tower Lock #2

Each light tower runs on 53 watts. Evaluate the power to turn on the towers. Evalúa la potencia 53.

💡 Hint: 53 = 5 × 5 × 5. First 5×5 = 25, then 25×5 = 125.
Sentence frame: “The power 24 means 2 times itself ____ times, which equals ____. The power 53 equals ____.”
🔒 Finish Act 1 first to unlock Act 2.
Act 2 · Evaluate & Write Expressions

Stage & Lights

🎭 Stage Cost — Order of Operations

The stage costs 3 + 4 × 5 coins. Use the order of operations: do multiply before add. What is the cost? Evalúa 3 + 4 × 5. Multiplica antes de sumar.

💡 Hint: First multiply: 4 × 5 = 20. Then add: 3 + 20 = 23.

💡 Light Budget — Write the Expression

Each light costs 8 coins. You buy n lights, plus a flat 20 coin setup fee. Write the expression for the total cost. Escribe la expresión: 8 coins por cada luz (n luces) más 20 de cuota fija.

💡 Hint: “8 coins for each of n lights” means 8 × n = 8n. Then add the 20 fee: 8n + 20.
Sentence frame: “3 + 4 × 5 equals ____ because I multiply first. ‘8 coins for each of n lights plus a 20 fee’ is the expression ____.”
🔒 Finish Act 2 first to unlock the Final Show.
Final Show · Boss

The Headliner

🎤 Master Switch — Power AND Expression

The big show needs BOTH skills. The master power is 23 + 6 × 4. Evaluate it: do the power first, then multiply, then add. What is the total? Evalúa 23 + 6 × 4 (potencia, luego multiplicar, luego sumar).

💡 Hint: 23 = 8. Then 6 × 4 = 24. Last, add: 8 + 24 = 32.
Sentence frame: “First 23 = ____. Then 6 × 4 = ____. Last, ____ + ____ = ____.”

Show Complete!

You powered up the whole festival! You used powers and exponents for the speakers and lights, and expressions to plan the costs. The crowd is cheering. Amazing work, Producer Mika!

¡Encendiste todo el festival! Usaste potencias y expresiones. ¡Excelente trabajo!
