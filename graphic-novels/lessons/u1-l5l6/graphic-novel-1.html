Decimal Docking Bay

The station's fuel computer is glitching! Only correct decimal math can dock the supply ship. Line up those decimal points and save the crew, Cadet.

¡La computadora de combustible falla! Solo las matematicas con decimales pueden acoplar la nave. Alinea los puntos decimales y salva a la tripulacion.

Act 1 · Lesson 1-5

Fuel Totals

⛽ Fuel Lock #1 — Add Decimals

Add the two tanks: 3.4 + 2.75. Line up the decimal points. What is the total?Suma los dos tanques: 3.4 + 2.75. Alinea los puntos. ¿Cuál es el total?

💡 Hint: Annex a zero so both have 2 places: 3.40 + 2.75. Add hundredths, tenths, then ones.
Sentence frame: “I lined up the decimal points and added to get ____ liters.”

⛽ Fuel Lock #2 — Subtract Decimals

A leak drains fuel. Start with 8.5 liters and subtract 3.27 liters. How much is left?Una fuga drena combustible. De 8.5 litros resta 3.27. ¿Cuánto queda?

💡 Hint: Annex a zero: 8.50 - 3.27. Line up the points and subtract place by place.
Sentence frame: “After lining up the points, 8.50 - 3.27 = ____ liters.”

Act 2 · Lesson 1-6

Power Boost

⚡ Power Lock #1 — Multiply Decimals

Set the reactor: 0.6 × 0.4. Multiply, then place the decimal point. What is the product?Ajusta el reactor: 0.6 × 0.4. Multiplica y coloca el punto. ¿Cuál es el producto?

💡 Hint: 6 × 4 = 24. There is 1 decimal place in 0.6 and 1 in 0.4 — that's 2 places total, so the product has 2 places.
Sentence frame: “0.6 has 1 place and 0.4 has 1 place, so the product 0.24 has ____ decimal places.”

⚡ Power Lock #2 — Place the Point

Fine-tune the power: 1.2 × 0.05. Multiply, then place the decimal point correctly.Ajuste fino: 1.2 × 0.05. Multiplica y coloca bien el punto.

💡 Hint: 12 × 5 = 60. Places: 1 (in 1.2) + 2 (in 0.05) = 3. You may need to annex a zero in front.
Sentence frame: “1.2 (1 place) × 0.05 (2 places) needs ____ decimal places, so the product is 0.06.”
🔒 Finish the previous chapter first to unlock this one.
Final · Lessons 1-5 & 1-6

The Launch Code

🔒 Launch Code — Add then Multiply

Compute (4.5 + 1.5) × 0.5. Add first, then multiply. Which line is fully correct?Calcula (4.5 + 1.5) × 0.5. Suma primero, luego multiplica. ¿Cuál línea es correcta?

💡 Hint: 4.5 + 1.5 = 6.0. Then 6.0 × 0.5 = 3.0 (half of 6).
Sentence frame: “4.5 + 1.5 = ____, and that sum × 0.5 = ____.”
