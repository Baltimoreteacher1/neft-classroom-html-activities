Decimal Division Drive

The hyperdrive splits long decimal distances into equal jumps. To divide with decimals, make the divisor a whole number first. Pilot us home, Cadet!

🚀 Jump Lock #1 — Divide by a Decimal

Compute 4.5 ÷ 0.5. Make the divisor a whole number first. How many jumps?Calcula 4.5 ÷ 0.5. Haz el divisor entero primero. ¿Cuántos saltos?

💡 Hint: Multiply both by 10: 4.5 ÷ 0.5 = 45 ÷ 5 = 9.
Sentence frame: “I multiplied both numbers by 10, so 4.5 ÷ 0.5 = 45 ÷ 5 = ____.”
