Act 2 · Mean, Median, Mode & MAD

Center & Spread

🎯 Insight #1 — When the Mean Lies

A scout's salary offers (in $1000s) are 10, 11, 12, 13, 54. The mean is 20 (sum 100 ÷ 5), but the median is 12. Which measure better describes a typical offer, and why?

🎯 Insight #2 — Compute the MAD

Player A scored 4, 6, 8, 10, 2. The mean is 6. Compute the mean absolute deviation: average the distance of each value from the mean. What is the MAD?

🎯 Insight #3 — Interpret the Spread

Two guards both average 10 points. Guard X has a MAD of 1.2; Guard Y has a MAD of 4.8. For a must-have-points possession, who is more consistent?
