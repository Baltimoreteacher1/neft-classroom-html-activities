Court Vision: The Data Analyst

You are Maya, the new data analyst for the team! The coach needs your help. Read the data, find the mean, median, and mode, and make the right call to win the game!

Eres Maya, la analista de datos del equipo. Lee los datos y haz la jugada correcta para ganar el partido.

Act 1 · Statistical Questions & Data

The Big Question

📊 Decision #1 — Pick a Statistical Question

A statistical question has many different answers you collect as data. Which question is statistical? Una pregunta estadística tiene muchas respuestas diferentes. ¿Cuál lo es?

💡 Hint: If everyone would give the same one answer, it is NOT statistical. If answers vary (change from game to game), it IS statistical.

📊 Decision #2 — Read the Data

Here is Jordan's points data for 5 games. Read the table. How many points did Jordan score in Game 3? Lee la tabla. ¿Cuántos puntos anotó en el Juego 3?

Jordan — Points per Game
Game 1 2 3 4 5
Points 8 12 10 6 14
💡 Hint: Find the column labeled Game 3, then look at the Points row right below it.
Sentence frame: “A statistical question is statistical because the answers ____ (vary / stay the same). In Game 3, Jordan scored ____ points.”

Act 2 · Mean, Median & Mode

Center of the Game

🎯 Stat Lock #1 — The Mean (Average)

Jordan's points were 8, 12, 10, 6, 14. The mean is the average: add them all, then divide by how many games. What is the mean? La media: suma todos los puntos y divide entre el número de juegos.

💡 Hint: 8 + 12 + 10 + 6 + 14 = 50. There are 5 games. Now do 50 ÷ 5.

🎯 Stat Lock #2 — The Median (Middle)

Put the points in order from least to greatest: 6, 8, 10, 12, 14. The median is the middle number. What is the median? La mediana es el número del medio cuando están en orden.

💡 Hint: With 5 numbers in order, the middle one is the 3rd number: 6, 8, 10, 12, 14.

🎯 Stat Lock #3 — The Mode (Most Often)

In a new game series, Maya wrote the assists: 7, 7, 9, 4, 7. The mode is the number that appears most often. What is the mode? La moda es el número que aparece más veces.

💡 Hint: Count each number. 7 appears three times. Every other number appears once.
Sentence frame: “The mean is ____. The median (middle) is ____. The mode (most often) is ____.”
🔒 Finish Act 2 first to unlock the Final Call.
Final Call · Boss

The Game-Winning Decision

🏆 Coach's Call — Who Should Take the Shot?

Coach asks for the most reliable scorer. Maya's last 5 shots scored: 5, 5, 5, 7, 3. Find the mean AND the mode, then pick the line that is fully correct. Encuentra la media Y la moda. Elige la línea correcta.

💡 Hint: Sum = 5+5+5+7+3 = 25, and 25 ÷ 5 = mean. The number that repeats most is the mode.
Sentence frame: “The mean is ____. The mode is ____. So Maya is a reliable scorer because her scores stay near ____.”
