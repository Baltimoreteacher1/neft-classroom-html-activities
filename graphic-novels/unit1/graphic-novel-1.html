Prime Station: The Factor Code

You are a space cadet. The station is broken. The doors are locked. Only factor codes can open them. Use your math to save the station!

Eres un cadete espacial. La estación está dañada. Usa las matemáticas para abrir las puertas.

Act 1 · Lesson 1-1

The Locked Bay

🔐 Airlock Lock #1

The panel needs a prime number. A prime number has only two factors: 1 and itself. Which number is prime? ¿Cuál número es primo (solo tiene 2 factores)?

💡 Hint: Try to break each number into a product like 2×something. If you cannot (except 1×itself), it is prime.

🔐 Airlock Lock #2 — the Factor Code

Break 36 all the way down into prime numbers. Pick the option where every end of the tree is a prime: 36 = ? Elige el árbol de factores correcto para 36.

💡 Hint: 36 = 6 × 6. Then break each 6 into 2 × 3. Keep going until every branch ends in a prime number.
Sentence frame: “The number ____ is prime because it has only ____ factors. The prime factorization of 36 is ____ × ____ × ____ × ____.”
🔒 Finish Act 1 first to unlock Act 2.
Act 2 · Lesson 1-2

The Twin Engines

⚙️ Sync Lock #1 — Common Factors

Engine A needs power level 12. Engine B needs 18. To sync them, find a number that divides both (a common factor). Which is a common factor of 12 and 18? ¿Cuál número divide a 12 y a 18 (factor común)?

💡 Hint: Factors of 12: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12. Factors of 18: 1, 2, 3, 6, 9, 18. Look for one in both lists.

⚙️ Sync Lock #2 — the GCF

Now share the fuel fairly. The crates hold 24 and 36 units. Find the Greatest Common Factor (GCF) — the biggest number that divides both. Encuentra el Máximo Factor Común (el factor común más grande) de 24 y 36.

💡 Hint: Common factors of 24 and 36 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12. The greatest one is the GCF.
Sentence frame: “A common factor of 12 and 18 is ____. The GCF of 24 and 36 is ____ because it is the biggest number that divides both.”
🔒 Finish Act 2 first to unlock the Final Code.
Final Code · Boss

The Master Door

🔒 Master Door — the Combined Code

The boss door needs BOTH skills. First the prime factorization of 30, then the GCF of 30 and 45. Pick the line that is fully correct. Elige la línea correcta: factorización de 30 y el MFC de 30 y 45.

💡 Hint: 30 = 2 × 3 × 5. Factors of 30: 1,2,3,5,6,10,15,30. Factors of 45: 1,3,5,9,15,45. The biggest shared one is the GCF.
Sentence frame: “30 = ____ × ____ × ____. The GCF of 30 and 45 is ____.”
