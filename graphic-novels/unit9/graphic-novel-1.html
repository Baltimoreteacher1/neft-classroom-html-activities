Treasure Map Navigator

You are Maya, a young explorer. You found an old treasure map! To reach the treasure, you must plot points on a grid and use integers (numbers above and below zero). Let’s go!

Eres Maya, una joven exploradora. Para llegar al tesoro, debes marcar puntos en una cuadrícula y usar números enteros (arriba y abajo de cero).

Act 1 · Graph on the Coordinate Plane

The Map Grid

📍 Map Point #1 — Which Quadrant?

The map shows the point (−4, 3). The first number is x (left/right). The second is y (up/down). Which quadrant is it in? El punto (−4, 3): x es izquierda/derecha, y es arriba/abajo. ¿En qué cuadrante está?

💡 Hint: x = −4 means go LEFT. y = 3 means go UP. Left + Up is the top-left, which is Quadrant II.

📍 Map Point #2 — Plot the Path

From the center (0, 0), the clue says go 3 right and 2 down. Which ordered pair is that? Desde (0,0): 3 a la derecha y 2 hacia abajo. ¿Cuál par ordenado es?

💡 Hint: Right is positive x. Down is negative y. So x = 3 and y = −2.
Sentence frame: “The point (−4, 3) is in Quadrant ____ because x is ____ and y is ____. Going 3 right and 2 down is the point ( ____ , ____ ).”
🔒 Finish Act 1 first to unlock Act 2.
Act 2 · Integers & Absolute Value

Above & Below the Sea

🏔️ Elevation Lock — Order the Levels

The clues are at these heights: 6 m up, −8 m (below sea level), 0 m (sea level), 12 m up, and −15 m deep. Put them in order from lowest to highest. Ordena de menor a mayor: 6, −8, 0, 12, −15.

💡 Hint: On a number line, numbers get bigger to the right. Negative numbers are below zero. The most negative (deepest) is the smallest.

🌊 Depth Lock — How Far From the Surface?

Maya dives to −20 m (20 meters below sea level). How far is she from the surface (0)? This is the absolute value, written |−20|. ¿Qué tan lejos de la superficie está a −20 m? Eso es el valor absoluto |−20|.

💡 Hint: Absolute value is the distance from 0. It is never negative. |−20| = 20.
Sentence frame: “From lowest to highest the order is ____. The diver at −20 m is ____ meters from the surface because |−20| = ____.”
🔒 Finish Act 2 first to unlock the Final.
Final · Navigate to the Treasure

X Marks the Spot

👑 Treasure Lock — The Final Clue

The treasure is at point (−2, −5), buried 5 m below sea level. Pick the line that is fully correct: the quadrant of the point AND the distance of −5 from the surface. Elige la línea correcta: el cuadrante de (−2, −5) y la distancia de −5 desde 0.

💡 Hint: x = −2 (left) and y = −5 (down) → bottom-left = Quadrant III. And |−5| = 5.
Sentence frame: “The point (−2, −5) is in Quadrant ____. The chest is ____ meters from the surface because |−5| = ____.”
