Master Chef Kitchen: The Recipe Rescue

The flagship restaurant Aurora is slammed on its biggest night, and every locked station now answers only to precise fraction math. As the rising chef Kai, you must interpret division of fractions, portion ingredients, and scale recipes under pressure — before the dinner service collapses. Watch for the optional ⭐ Chef’s Challenge in each act for extra credit.

Act 1 · Lesson 2-1

The Locked Pantry

🍲 Order Ticket — Interpret the Model

The reduction simmering on the pass measures 5 cups, and each tasting spoon takes 1/3 cup. Which statement correctly interprets 5 ÷ 1/3?

🔐 Pantry Cipher — Solve Precisely

To release the pantry, portion 7/8 cup of glaze into 1/4-cup brushes. Evaluate 7/8 ÷ 1/4 and report it as a mixed number.

⭐ Chef’s Challenge

Optional extra credit — the pantry is already open. Try it, or skip ahead to Act 2 any time.

The Reverse Recipe

A chef divided a batch of dressing into 1/6-cup ramekins and filled exactly 9 of them. How much dressing was there to start? (Think: what total ÷ 1/6 = 9?)

Act 2 · Lesson 2-2

The Big Order

🍳 Service Lock — Portioning the Pot

The banquet pot holds 9 cups of risotto and each entrée plates 3/4 cup. How many full entrées can Kai send? Evaluate 9 ÷ 3/4.

🥐 Scaling Lock — Stretch the Recipe

A sauce recipe needs 2/3 cup of cream, but Kai only wants to make 1/6-cup tasting portions for the judges. How many tasting portions does one recipe yield? Evaluate 2/3 ÷ 1/6.

⭐ Chef’s Challenge

Optional extra credit — the order is already sent. Solve it or jump straight to the Final Dish.

The Mixed-Number Scale-Down

Kai has 2 1/2 cups of broth and needs 5/8-cup bowls. How many bowls can be filled exactly? Evaluate 2 1/2 ÷ 5/8.

🔒 Complete Act 2 to unlock the Final Dish.
Final Dish · Boss

The Banquet

🍽️ The Banquet Cipher — Combined Order

The head chef hands Kai the master ticket. It needs ONE fully correct line: first portion 3 3/4 cups of bisque into 1/2-cup cups, then confirm how many 2/3-cup ladles fill 6 cups. Pick the line where BOTH results are right.

⭐ Chef’s Final Challenge

Optional extra credit — the banquet is already plated. Take the bow now, or crack this last puzzle first.

The Leftover Reallocation

After service, 3 1/3 cups of soup remain. Kai pours them into 5/6-cup staff bowls. How many full bowls, and how much soup is left over?
